The mother of Modern Family star Ariel Winter has denied reports that she tried to leak photos of her older daughter Shanelle Gray, 34,

Chrystal Workman had been accused of attempting to release the photos in revenge after Shanelle won temporary custody of Ariel, 14.

But on Thursday Chrystal told TMZ from her home in Los Angeles: 'I have no naked photos on my daughter, no naked photos.'

Not true: Chrystal Workman, the mother of Ariel Winters, told TMZ on Thursday that she had not tried to leak naked pictures of her other daughter Chanelle Gray to the press

Allegations: A Hollywood publicist has come forward to claim that Ariel's estranged mother tried to enlist his help in leaking the photos of Shanelle. Pictured outside her LA home on Thursday

Chrystal lost custody of Ariel after she was accused of 'emotionally and physically abusing' her.

On Wednesday Hollywood publicist called Jonathan Hay told  TMZ that Chrystal had approached him earlier in the week claiming to have snaps she wanted to leak to the press.

However Hay said he refused to help because he felt she was trying to 'sell out her daughter'.

Drama: The 14-year-old Modern Family star is currently in the custody of her big sister after allegations that her mother Chrystal Workman physically and mentally abused her. Pictured in July at the Greek Theatre in LA

He also claims Chrystal 'hounded' him all week about release the pictures - which he says are eight or nine years old - but he didn't want any part of the sleazy plan because he apparently wants to help Ariel.

Hay says he's already been contacted by a member of Ariel's legal team but added at this point it's unclear how much of a role, if any, he could play in the custody case.

He told TMZ he would have no problem filing a declaration in support of Ariel but says he has not been asked to do so yet.

In October Ariel, who plays Alex Dunphy in the hit show, was removed from her mother's custody amid allegations that Chrystal was such a 'nightmare' on the set of Modern Family she almost caused Ariel to lose her job.

Head down: Chrystal refused to answer reporters' questions

Chrystal is currently not allowed any contact with Ariel but vehemently denies any wrongdoing.

Ariel's brother Jimmy Workman has also supported his mother in the wake of the claims, telling Us Weekly: 'There is no truth to these allegations.'

Although Shanelle was appointed as a temporary guardian she does not have access to Winter's earnings, according to the judge's order.

The TV actress is the voice of 'Wendy' in the burger chain's adverts, but took a career break following her marriage to Pepsi heir David Barry Gray, with whom she has two daughters.

Ariel is believed to be raking in roughly $75,000 for Modern Family has been acting since age seven.

She appeared in several TV series, including ER and Phineas and Ferb, and movies such as Kiss Kiss Bang Bang, Ice Age: The Meltdown and ParaNorman

Ariel's case will be back in court on December 10.
