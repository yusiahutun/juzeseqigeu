Someone Needs to Buy This Adorable $39,000 North Carolina Church

March 31, 2024
blog

It's not just the Greek Revival facade of this cute-as-a-button church for sale in the historic district of Woodville, North Carolina that has us drooling with house envy. The interior, too, is more than worth a peek—it's been perfectly restored to its original 1847 aesthetic. It's the kind of character you don't comes across everyday, and for which you'd expect to pay an arm and leg. But you know we love a good real estate deal, especially one this beautiful.

Nancy Winslow

Ready for the price? $39,000.

Nancy Winslow

According to Nancy Winslow of Keller Williams Realty, the rural, 2.29-acre lot on which it sits is the church's original location. But for much of its life, it lived a mile down the road in Lewiston, where it was moved in 1896. Its eventual return to Woodville fulfilled the lifelong dream of Lillian Boyette, who rode in the church as a young girl during its initial move and longed to see it return to its rightful place. Thanks to her friend Elizabeth Widmer and Historic Woodville, St. Francis Methodist Church came home in 2001, thereby reuniting it with the neighboring cemetery—which is also part of the sale.

The church's zoning would allow for a single family residence or commercial business, though it does come with a number of historical covenants to ensure the preservation of its most significant features.
