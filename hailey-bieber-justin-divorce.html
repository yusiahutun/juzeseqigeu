Hailey Bieber Responds to Justin Bieber Divorce Rumors

April 29, 2024
blog

Speaking out her truth! Hailey Bieber just responded to all the Justin divorce rumors floating around. The model posted a story that was directed to all her haters and those who want her downfall.

The celebrity power couple go way back and was introduced back in 2009 by Hailey’s dad, the actor Stephen Baldwin. However, Justin was with Selena Gomez at the time. Hailey and Justin met again in 2011, but it wasn’t until 2016 during one of Justin’s breaks from Selena that rumors of a budding romance between Justin and Hailey began to circulate. At the end of 2015, Hailey and Justin’s flirty interactions were documented on Instagram. It all came to a head when Justin shared a photo of locking lips with Hailey in 2016 and their romance was seemingly confirmed.

After a short breakup and reunion, Justin proposed during an intimate vacation to the Bahamas. Justin shared the news via Instagram after the tabloids had picked up on the story, noting in a very long caption: “Was gonna wait a while to say anything but word travels fast, listen plain and simple Hailey I am soooo in love with everything about you! So committed to spending my life getting to know every single part of you loving you patiently and kindly.” They married in a courthouse wedding in September 2018, just four months after reuniting and two months after announcing their engagement. A year later, they celebrated their love again at their second wedding in South Carolina.

So with all their history and nearly six years together as a married couple, are the divorce rumors true? Here’s if Justin and Hailey Bieber are actually divorcing.

Are Justin and Hailey Bieber divorcing?

No, Justin and Hailey Bieber are not divorcing. Sources close to the couple disclosed to People that the two are super amicable. “There’s no divorce and no truth to that whatsoever,” the insider says. “They are very, very happy.”

Hailey also took to Instagram days to put an end to the rumors once and for all. “Just FYI the stories and constant ‘blind items’ I see on TikTok are 100% of the time wrong Made out of thin air… Come from the land of delusion…” she wrote in an Instagram story. “So I know it may be fun feeding into these stories but just know they’re always false xx sorry to spoil it.”

Rumors started to emerge of a breakup when Hailey’s father Stephen Baldwin asked fans if they could pray for his daughter and son-in-law. The actor posted a video of the song, “I Could Sing of Your Love Forever” by Delirious? and Hillsong Worship. “Christians, please when you think of Justin & Hailey, take a moment to offer a little prayer for them to have wisdom, protection and to draw close to the Lord,” the clip’s text read, per Page Six.

Speaking of in-laws, the Rhode founder made a touching post for Justin’s mom, Pattie Mallette’s birthday. “Happy birthday to my beautiful Mama in law,” she captioned a selfie of the two on Instagram Stories on Wednesday, April 3, 2024. “Having another amazing Mom is all anyone could ask for.”
