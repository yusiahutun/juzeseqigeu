Jessica Lowndes Biography

Jessica Lowndes was born in Vancouver , British Columbia, on November 8, 1988. She's a Canadian singer and actress. She is best known for her role as Adrianna Tate-Duncan in 90210 The CW teen drama series and for appearing in several Hallmark Channel movies. In 2008, she also played Mandi in the Greek series. She appeared on America's Next Top Model as a guest in 2009.

Jessica Lowndes Early Life

Lowndes attended private school, which she later learned wasn't her. She knew from an early age that she cherished singing and acting. Growing up in a household where her mother was a piano teacher and her father kept a teenage record of running across Canada, Lowndes knew it was possible to achieve a goal and make her dreams come true.

While Lowndes is known for singing and dancing all the time as a young child, she finally got her first big acting opportunity in high school. Lowndes also produced much of her own early music. Lowndes worked on a Showtime project during the summer before her senior year and instantly knew she wanted to work on-screen one day. Lowndes met people from LA and had representation in the entertainment industry during their work on this project.
