Meaning of Real Sisters by Future

July 14, 2024
blog

The Future song titled "Real Sisters" delves into a theme that can be described as a mix of relationships, loyalty, and the streets. In this lyrical journey, Future explores the complexities of his interactions with women while also highlighting the gritty realities of his past life in the streets. Let's dissect some of the standout lyrics and the imaginative themes they help to develop within the song.The opening lines immediately set the tone for the song, as Future declares his presence in the new wave of music. This establishes his confidence and his desire to push boundaries. The repetition of "that's your shit" adds a touch of swagger and assertiveness, signaling that he is unapologetic about pursuing what he wants.In the chorus, Future alludes to his indulgence in intoxicants while expressing a desire to engage in these activities with someone else. This lyric echoes the themes of camaraderie, shared experiences, and a hedonistic lifestyle. It suggests that shared experiences can strengthen relationships, even if it means exploring unconventional connections.One of the most thought-provoking lyrics of the song is when Future encounters two women who claim to be real sisters. He reveals that their familial bond doesn't deter his interest in exploring a romantic or sexual relationship with both of them. This lyric examines societal norms surrounding relationships and challenges the notion of sisterhood as an impenetrable barrier. It explores the unconventional idea of introducing desires and attractions within a supposedly sacred bond.Another compelling lyric highlights Future's life in the streets, as he boasts about selling heroin despite the risks involved. This line portrays the dark and dangerous side of his past life, revealing his audacity and fearlessness. It delves into the theme of survival and the lengths one goes to succeed in a harsh environment. Additionally, it exposes the flawed understanding of loyalty by illustrating Future's willingness to engage in illegal activities.The chorus is repeated several times throughout the song, emphasizing its significance and the interconnectedness of the themes discussed. It reinforces the idea that loyalty isn't confined to superficial or traditional relationships, but rather can exist within unconventional dynamics.Moving into the second verse, Future references his affinity for luxury and the allure of material possessions. He mentions his fondness for Hublot watches, symbolizing his success and his willingness to invest in opulence. This theme explores the inner conflicts within someone who rises to fame and fortune from humble beginnings. It questions the impact of wealth on one's identity and values.Furthermore, he talks about taking drugs and rocking designer brands, such as Tommy Hilfiger and Tom Ford. This line blurs the boundaries between reality and illusion, signifying his detachment from the mundane and his immersion in a dreamlike state. It examines the theme of escapism, where substances and material possessions temporarily transport individuals away from their troubles.To wrap up the song, Future reiterates the chorus, concluding the narrative. The repetition emphasizes the central themes of loyalty, shared experiences, and the blurred lines between relationships. It reinforces the idea that one's loyalty is not always bound by societal expectations or conventional norms."Real Sisters" manages to explore various imaginative and unexpected themes, all circling back to the overarching theme of complex relationships and loyalty. Through powerful lyrics and evocative storytelling, Future pushes the boundaries of conventional thinking and challenges listeners to ponder the intricacies of human connections. It is an invitation to question societal norms, explore unconventional relationships, and reflect upon the complexities of loyalty within various contexts.
