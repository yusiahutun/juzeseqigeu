There would be no great hair scenes in the country with no Marley braids. They give any lady a modish, beautiful, and chic look at all time especially when they style them with trendy as well as unique hairstyles. These braids are considered to be the best for any occasion as they have different styles and fashion one can rock in. Below are some of the best Marley braids hairstyles

1. Crotchet Marley braids

If you are an impatient lady, you should consider this Marley braid hairstyle. They are very convenient in terms of saving your precious time. You will need about 1 to 2 hours to fix them and have that natural and cute look. Your stylist will first create cornrow lines to your natural hair and then use a crotchet needle to fix them. Remember to remind your stylist not to plait your hairline to avoid hair breakage. You should also tell him or her to crotchet them gently to avoid straining the scalp. Long crotchet braids Marley hair have so many styling techniques. You have very different trendy looks on daily basis. The short ones are also available though they have minimal styles.

You can also wash them without necessarily un-twisting and re-twisting them.

14. Marley twist low updo

This style is so very beautiful and easy to get it done. All you have to do is to part your hair at the middle of your head. Twist one section over the back and do the same to the other section. Take both the ends, braid them together, and tuck them at the back of the braid. Simple yet classy. Right?

Simple steps on how to do Marley braids hairstyles

To achieve the impressive look that comes with Marley braids is usually time consuming though the effort is worth it. Here we will offer you step-by-step way on how do Marley twist braids on your own. What you need is Marley braiding extension, shampoo, conditioner, water, a comb, and hair gel.

  • Step 1 – Soak the Marley hair extension and then dry it well. Can Marley braids get wet? Well, if you soak them for some few minutes, they will get wet.
  • Step 2 – If your hair is not clean, kindly wash it as well with a shampoo. Condition your hair for easy combing, treat it to avoid breakage, and use a blow dry to dry it well.
  • Step 3 – To detangle your hair completely, make sure you comb it well so that you can easily part into sections.
  • Step 4 – What size do you prefer for your Marley twist braids? Well, part it considering your preferred size into sections.
  • Step 5 – Apply hair gel on your start section, take a chunk of the Marley braiding extension, place it well around your hair, and braid them strongly together.
  • Step 6 – After twisting it well until the end, dip the twist end into boiling water carefully to seal it.
  • Step 7 – Do the same to all the sections.

How to maintain Marley braids hairstyles

If you want your Marley braids to serve you for some weeks, you have to maintain them. The best part is that they require minimal maintenance.
