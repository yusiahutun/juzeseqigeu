Napa Valley Wine Train

California is for lovers, and so is this idyllic experience. No date night can compare to the Napa Valley Wine Train's 'Romance on the Rails' journey. Dine in a private booth in the glass topped Vista Dome, with stars twinkling above as you cruise through the vineyards. Sierra Dinner Train

The third oldest railroad in North America features scenes so picturesque, it's a popular spot for filming movies. Take in gorgeous views of Northern California's Sierra Mountains as you enjoy drinks and a three-course meal. After dinner, move out to the open-air car to capture memorable photos.

Rio Grande Scenic Railroad

Wind and curve through mountain meadows and keep an eye out for elk, bears, and eagles as you pass by. Enjoy cocktails and a delicious meal on the romantic sunset dinner ride, accompanied by stunning views of Colorado's peaks. They don't call it scenic for nothing!
