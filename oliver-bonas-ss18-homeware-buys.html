10 pieces you'll love from Oliver Bonas' new homeware collection

October 09, 2024
blog

We need these on-trend home accessories now!

    With snuggly cushions, luxe velvet armchairs and chic barware, Oliver Bonas' new SS18 home collection is even more covetable than ever. The brand has launched hundreds of stylish new home accessories and furnishings ahead of spring-summer, and trust us when we say you're going to love them!

    We've rounded up ten of the key pieces to look out for, from this incredible bar to budget-friendly photo frames and cushions. While some are already available in stores and online, others won't be on sale until March or April, giving you plenty of time to prepare your wish list. Happy shopping!

    MORE: Get inspiration from these celebrity homes

    Luxe Marble & Glass Drinks Stand, £435

    If your Pinterest boards are filled with images of perfectly-styled bar carts and trolleys, you're going to love this marble and glass drinks stand. Not only does it have plenty of bar space for mixing and serving cocktails, but there are also shelves to display your glasses and bottles so you'll look like a true pro! Due in stores in April.

    Photo: Oliver Bonas

    Uroso Mulberry Armchair, £595

    We don't know what we love more, the rich berry tone of this armchair or its luxe fabric… either way, it's sure to become the focal point of any room! Expected in stores in January.

    Photo: Oliver Bonas

    Velvet and sheepskin cushions, £28-£50

    Changing your cushions is one of the easiest ways to give your home an instant update, and these are a sure-fire hit. The only challenge is choosing between the luxe velvet designs or the fluffy sheepskin cushions. Due in stores in January.

    Photo: Oliver Bonas

    Nappa Table Lamp, £130

    The 70s are having an interiors style revival, and this pink tasselled table lamp is an easy way to try the trend. Feeling bold? Snap up the complementing tasselled mirror for a true style statement. In stores now.

    Photo: Oliver Bonas

    Cupola Table Lamp, £80

    Two of our interiors obsessions – metallic and marble – combine in the shape of this curved table lamp, which has a berry toned lampshade that gives it even more style points. Due in stores in February.

    Photo: Oliver Bonas

    Sheepskin rugs, £80

    Available in muted tones of cream, stone and pink, these fluffy sheepskin rugs are bound to be popular with anyone hoping to incorporate Scandinavian-inspired styling into their homes. Due in stores in January.

    Photo: Oliver Bonas

    Ceramic vases, £24-£28

    Add a pop of colour to your room with these decorative vases, available in washed green, orange and blue tones. The perfect decorative pieces to invest in for spring. Expected in stores in March.

    Photo: Oliver Bonas

    Ceilo Table Lamp, £55

    Your desk or bedside table is calling out for one of these contemporary table lamps, featuring exposed filament bulbs and available in gold, rose gold and black. We want all three! Due in stores in February.

    Photo: Oliver Bonas

    Tassel cushions, £32

    Kitsch and colourful, these tassel cushions will brighten up your sofa or bed in time for spring. Take your pick from three bright motifs. Due in stores in January.

    Photo: Oliver Bonas

    Pinboard range, £35-£65

    Create an Instagram-worthy desk or home office setup with these pinboards, featuring complementing tones of midnight blue, coral and sage green. Expected in stores in February.

    Photo: Oliver Bonas
