An young wife has celebrated the repayment of her bride price on social media. The lady, identified as Uka Chiemela, shared a video from the occasion on TikTok and got many people talking.

She asked netizens to share how it is done in their hometowns. In the clip, tubers of yams, biscuits and other items were arranged in a corner.
