Shannen and Australian actor Julian McMahon (b. July 27, 1968) met on the set of the “Charmed” TV series. His first appearance was on the 3rd season, “The Honeymoon’s Over” episode, aired on October 5, 2000. After the first eight episodes on the season (aired between October 5 and December 14, 2000), he would guest star again from episode 13th to 16th, and from 19th to the last one, the 22nd, airing between February 8, 2001 and May 17, 2001. They also worked together in the supernatural film “Another Day”, released on December 4, 2001.
