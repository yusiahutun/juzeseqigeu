AJ Ross Career

AJ began her career in the field of Journalism. In January 2006 she worked as a Production Assistant for two years. After two years she left the job and worked with WMBF. In February 2018 she started working as a Sports Reporter for CBS Sports. She has worked with them for more than 3 years in the Greater New York City area.
