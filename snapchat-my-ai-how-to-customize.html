How to Customize Your Snapchat My AI

June 26, 2024
blog

Snapchat's My AI is a chatbot that you can chat with about anything and it will respond in a friendly way. The best part is you can customize its appearance to match your style.

Whether you want your AI companion to reflect your identity or you just want to experiment with different looks, it's easy to personalize your Snapchat AI. Here's how to change the name, gender, outfit, and bio of your Snapchat My AI.

How to Customize Your Snapchat My AI

You can change everything about your Snapchat AI, including its name, gender, outfit, and bio, through its customization window. Let's first check out how to access the customization window.

Close
  • Open Snapchat and tap the Chat icon.
  • Tap My AI.
  • Tap My AI profile picture in the top left corner.

    • Now that you're in the customization window, let's personalize your Snapchat AI.

    How to Change My AI's Name and Gender on Snapchat

    To change your Snapchat My AI's name, tap on My AI name, type in a new name, and press Save.

    Close

    Similar to changing the chatbot's name, it's very easy to change your own Snapchat username.

    You can also change your Snapchat AI's gender to match your identity. To do this, tap the three horizontal dots in the top right corner of the screen and select Avatar settings.

    On the Start customising screen, tap on the avatar you want. The first avatar is female and the second avatar is male. Once you've chosen your avatar, tap Save in the top right corner.

    Close

    How to Change My AI's Outfit and Bio

    If you want to change the outfit of Snapchat's My AI, tap Customize and then Outfit. Choose an outfit of your choice, and then tap the Save button.

    Close

    Lastly, you can also add a bio for My AI that matches your personality. To do this, tap My AI's bio and type in a bio that matches your personality in the Bio field.

    You can also tap Randomize bio button to generate a random bio. Then, tap Save.

    Close

    Add a Personal Touch to Snapchat's My AI

    Snapchat's My AI has become a go-to place to kill free time. You can chat with it and ask questions you might hesitate to ask your friends. You can even customize it to your liking using the above steps.

    FAQ

    Q: How Do You Change the Background of Your AI on Snapchat?

    You can change your Snapchat AI's background by accessing My AI, tapping its name at the top, choosing Wallpaper, selecting Change Wallpaper, and choosing the new background you want to use. There are many chat wallpapers to choose from.

    Q: Is My AI a Real Person on Snapchat?

    No, My AI is not a real person on Snapchat. It's a chatbot powered by OpenAI, which uses artificial intelligence to answer your questions.

    Q: Is Snapchat AI Only for Apple?

    No, Snapchat's My AI chatbot is available for all users on both Android and iOS (iPhone) platforms. You can use the chatbot by opening your Snapchat app, tapping the message icon, choosing My AI, and sending your message to the bot.
