Love is Blinds AD follows Hailey Biebers footsteps as she wears creepy veil to wedding

April 13, 2024
blog

The veil worn at Love is Blind star AD’s wedding to Clay is “creeping” everyone out, with many convinced the ‘Til Death Do Us Part’ quote is going to scare her husband-to-be off.

Amber Desiree “AD” Smith falls for Clay Gravesande in the Love Is Blind pods on season 6, fully aware he’s a work in progress. However, she goes all out for their special day – with the creepiest veil ever, according to fans.

AD’s Love is Blind veil

AD wore a veil to her wedding to Clay with the words, “Til Death Do Us Part”. It’s giving Hailey Bieber, who wore the same words on her veil when she married Justin – but obviously, she’d known him for far longer.

She also had her new initials on the train of the dress too, to match the long, white veil. The wedding dress itself is from Savvy Bride Boutique, which is where co-star Amy got hers from too.

Samples from the Charlotte store’s sale cost $500 to $1,500, but the dresses range from $1,000 to $2,500. Veils at the store are Westwood Veil Collection as well as Bel Air Bridal, can range from $120 to $800. 

‘Til Death Do Us Part’ creeps out fans

The majority of Netflix viewers weren’t impressed with AD’s veil, but there’s a few who liked it.

“Ok wait because the veil AD picked was fire I loved it lol #LoveIsBlindS6,” said one fan.

“You know the producers were THRILLED AD chose that veil, for that shot for the trailer and that shot alone! lmao #LoveIsBlind #LoveIsBlindS6,” penned a social media user.

“That Til Death Do Us Part veil… Because that won’t freak Clay out anymore,” said a fan. While another said: “I wouldn’t even have that in my vows let alone on my veil. It’s a no for me.”

AD and Clay’s wedding – spoilers

During episodes 10 and 12 of Love is Blind, we saw AD – wearing her Til Death Do Us Part veil – and Clay on their wedding day, confirming they do indeed make it to the altar.

However, according to public records in Mecklenburg County, N.C., it appears that AD and Clay did not go through with their wedding. Despite this, the couple did sign a marriage license.
