Denise Van Outen cosies up in cute London-themed pyjamas

September 08, 2024
blog

What a perfect night in!

    Denise Van Outen looked incredibly cosy in a recent Instagram snap, as she snuggled up in a pair of London-themed pyjamas for a night in at a London hotel. The presenter, who posed on the edge of the bath in her luxurious-looking suite, looked perfectly content in her statement sleepwear, which came complete with a cute London transport print. “After a freezing cold day out on the golf course I watched the Brits in my cosy ‘London’ themed PJs,” she wrote alongside the picture, confirming that she’d opted to stay in and watch the 2018 BRIT Awards on television rather than attend the event.

    The cosy set is the London Print Pyjama from British loungewear line Cyberjammies, which is well known for its soft and cosy sleepwear. The woven long-sleeved ensemble is available for £48.00 from the brand’s website, and is even made in men’s and kids’ sizes too for a matching family look.

    Perhaps Denise was reminiscing about her recent skiing holiday - the trip to snowy Tignes surely called for some warming pyjamas to wrap up in during the evenings. Speaking exclusively to HELLO! at the time, Denise shared some of her personal photos from the trip, including a great snapshot of the view from her balcony, with the picturesque ski slopes visible in the background. "To be able to have that balcony and to look out, soak up the atmosphere and look at Tignes. It was so pretty, especially at night time when everything is lit up and you see the lights twinkling on the snow, it's lovely," Denise said.
