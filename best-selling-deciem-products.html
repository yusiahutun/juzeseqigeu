The best selling Deciem products, and how to receive a discount

July 17, 2024
blog

Canadian brand Deciem is as cult as cult can be in beauty. Celebrated by beauty editors, industry experts and all kinds of product lovers who simply value transparency, it's the "abnormal" beauty company that kick-started a shift in the way we consume skincare.

Combining cutting-edge science with modest price points, the late founder Brandon Truaxe clearly realised his mission to democratise serious skincare. In just four years he launched 10 brands under the Deciem umbrella — although all but two are currently being discontinued — and when a liquid foundation was released into budget range The Ordinary it was met with a 25,000-person waiting list. Not a PR story.

Deciem has never been one to conform, and this November the brand is launching a typically rebellious campaign to run across its two star brands: NIOD and The Ordinary. Designed to counteract the impulse purchases synonymous with Black Friday (which falls on the 25th of the month) Slowvember is a call to shop slowly, armed with the knowledge you really need to make the best choices for your skin. Every product has been reduced by 23 per cent for the entirety of November, encouraging fans to shop considerably and consciously.

Accompanying the discount is a month-long series of virtual and in-store experiences, designed to allow consumers the time and knowledge it takes to make educated, beneficial purchases.

If you're looking for a little inspiration before shopping (slowly, of course), see the story behind Deciem's most-popular products below...

The Ordinary Hyaluronic Acid 2% + B5

This combination of low, medium and high-weight molecules of hyaluronic acid gives an immediate moisture boost, leaving skin plumped and smooth. A great daily hydrator for all skin types.

The Ordinary Retinol 1% in Squalane

Steeped in skin-compatible emollient squalane, this retinol serum is potent enough to fade feathery lines, yet gentle enough not to induce irritation. Just don't forget your SPF in the morning.

NIOD Copper Amino Isolate Serum (CAIS)

This clever anti-ager contains 1.0% pure copper tripeptide-1, which you mix with an activator before the first use. It works on the skin's tone and texture, including pesky enlarged pores. It's deep blue in colour, but don't let that put you off.

NIOD Multi-Molecular Hyaluronic Complex

To be applied post-CAIS, or alone, this combines 12 forms of hyaluronic compounds and peptides. The Rolls-Royce of HA serums.

NIOD Re:Pigment

Uneven pigmentation is one of the trickiest skin issues to tackle topically, but NIOD's targetted treatment is up to the task. It uses a combination of peptides, acids and a white mushroom extract to lift dark spots and acne scars, working to brighten and unify the complexion in a matter of weeks.

The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1%

This watery serum is a brilliant one for anyone tackling overly oily skin and enlarged pores. The heavy concentration of niacinamide delivers multiple benefits, from strengthening the skin barrier to inhibiting excess sebum production, while the addition of zinc means it's able to help prevent breakouts, too.

The Ordinary Squalane Cleanser

This one won't replicate the soul-soothing properties of a spa-style balm or frothy cream, but it'll cleanse your complexion and leave your skin barrier balanced as well as a luxe alternative. What it lacks in sensory appeal, it more than makes up for in efficacy.

The Ordinary Natural Moisturizing Factors + HA

Another one to file under 'basic, but brilliant', this fragrance-free moisturiser is great for sealing in a serum, day or night. It's emollient-rich, yet won't block pores, and all skin types should get on well with it.
