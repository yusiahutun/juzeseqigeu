Florence Pugh, Carine Roitfeld, Kylie Jenner: The Best Dressed at Paris Couture Week SS24

July 08, 2024
blog

See all the outfits from A-listers on the front row...

    Paris Couture Week is back for 2024 and we couldn't be more excited.

    Granted, the Haute Couture runways bring fashion at its finest - mesmerising displays of wondrous designs and manufacturing at the highest level. But the celebrities who sit on the prestigious front rows equally step up their sartorial game and bring a feast for the eyes.

    It's hard to forget the avant-garde dress code of the VIPs last year - notably, Kylie Jenner's prosthetic lion head sat atop a slinky black maxi dress and Doja Cat (who arguably had the most impressive set of looks for the week) who stepped out at Schiaparelli adorned in 30,000 red Swarovski crystals in homage of Daniel Roseberry's show based on Dante Aligheiri’s book

    Schiaparelli once again opened the week with a bang. The show kicked off SS24 on Monday and drew in a crowd that included Jennifer Lopez, Zendaya and her costar sidekick Hunter Schafer, whilst Dior hosted Rihanna, Anya Taylor-Joy and Natalie Portman later that day - talk about starting as we mean to go on.

    Our question is, can any celebrity top the front row drama of SS23? See below the best dressed guests of Couture Week (so far)...

    Kylie Jenner & Carine Roitfeld

    At Jacquemus' Les Sculptures show (arguably the hottest ticket of PFW) legendary fashion editor Carine Roitfeld embodied chic in a fitted olive dress and statement necklace, while Kylie Jenner stunned in a vibrant red, figure-hugging dress with a bold shoulder cut-out. Both accessorised with oversized sunglasses (so fashion) at the Fondation Maeght in Saint-Paul-De-Vence on January 29.

    The Kardashian-Jenners

    Kris, Kim and Kylie were spotted arriving at the Maison Margiela  show on January 25. For the occasion Kris wore a large black coat and sunglasses. While Kim went for her signature style in a black and green cut-out dress with an elaborate necklace. Kylie continued her new quiet luxury aesthetic, eschewing her trademark Kardashian style code, in a form-fitting paillette clad dress and white heels.

    Monica Bellucci 

    Monica Bellucci captivated at the 'Diner de la Mode', during Paris Couture Fashion Week, in an elegant black velvet gown. The dress featured a plunging neckline and artful ruching, as well as a thigh-high slit. She paired the look with sheer black tights and patent leather heels, exuding timeless glamour at the Palais de Tokyo venue. 

    Zendaya

    Zendaya is making the razor fringe 2024's chicest hair trend and we can't get enough. She stunned in a plum ribbed dress at the Fendi show.

    Reese Witherspoon & Ava Phillippe

    The actress wore a black mini dress whilst her daughter (her doppelganger) opted for a yellow high-neck top layered with a grey blazer.

    Kylie Jenner & Stormi Webster

    The ultimate mother/daughter goals. Kylie and Stormi stepped out in matching black outfits with furry adornments and black sunglasses.

    Florence Pugh

    Flo oozed effortless elegance in a black one-shoulder gown with her blonde hair parted to one side.

    Rosie Huntington-Whiteley

    Rosie HW stunned in a sequinned maxi skirt, crop top and blazer at the Valentino show.

    Jennifer Lopez

    JLo stole the show at Elie Saab in an ethereal green dress with a statement floral cape, finished off with a feminine hair bow.

    Kris Jenner

     It was a family affair at the Valentino show. Corey Gamble and Kris Jenner joined her daughter and granddaughter in a grey textured suit paired with a white top-handle bag.

    Gwyneth Paltrow

    The Goop founder oozed sophistication in a black fitted half-sleeve shirt paired with velvet trousers at Armani.

    Camilla of Bourbon Two Sicilies

    The Italian aristocrat wore a black maxi dress featuring a sculped neckline, paired with silver statement earrings.

    Noah Cyrus

    The singer and sister of Miley Cyrus nailed sheer dressing in a textured see-through brown cape dress at the Stephane Rolland show.

    Florence Pugh

    Flo Pugh captured attention in a fleece zip-up jacket with suede brown open-toe sock boots and 60s-esque thick-rimmed sunglasses.

    Princess Maria Chiara and Princess Maria Carolina

    The Italian princesses stepped out at the Armani Privé show in the chicest ensembles. Maria Chiara wore a velvet maxi dress whilst Maria Carolina wore a red two-piece suit.

    Naomi Campbell

    Naomi nailed supermodel winter chic in a glitzy plum-hued tweed jacket at Chanel.

    Charlotte Casiraghi

    The Monégasque royal looked effortlessly elegant in a checked jacket, light jeans and Mary-Jane heels.

    Rosie Huntington-Whiteley

    The model and style icon arrived in Paris for couture in a black cape style blazer paired with leggings and sock boots.

    Princess Alexandra of Hanover

    Princess Alexandra stunned in a burnt orange maxi dress with cut-outs paired with court heels at Giambattista Valli.

    Philippine Leroy-Beaulieu

    The star oozed Parisian Chic in a fluffy animal-print coat at the Schiaparelli show.

    Natalie Portman

    Natalie Portman made the case for bare legs in winter in textured mini shorts, a satin waistcoat and a blazer over the top at the Dior show.

    Beatrice Borromeo

    The model and aristocrat oozed femininity in an opulent lace midi skirt paired with a sheer nude shirt and a cropped jacket.

    Jordyn Woods

    Jordyn stepped out at Siena Restaurant wearing a dopamine-inducing slinky blue maxi dress.

    Celeste

    The singer looked effortlessly glamorous in an animal print satin midi dress and black operatic gloves at the Dior show.

    Anya Taylor-Joy

    Anya stunned in a romantic red lace maxi dress with spaghetti straps paired with black chunky calf boots, giving the overall ensemble an edgier tone.

    Rihanna

    Rihanna wore a black quilted two-piece with a black Lady Dior, white stilettos and a baker boy-style hat that threw us straight back to the early 2000s.

    Elizabeth Debicki

    The Crown star nailed elevated cool in white pleated trousers paired with an off-the-shoulder white shirt.

    Kelly Rutherford

    Quiet luxury perfection. The Gossip Girl icon wore a jacket and A-line skirt two-piece paired with a Lady Dior bag and matching nude court heels.

    Zendaya

    Zendaya stunned in a fitted black bodysuit with a satin maxi skirt featuring statement draping from the back. She paired the look with fishnet knee-high tights and black court heels.

    Jennifer Lopez

    It's giving mob wife couture... JLo stepped out at Schiaparelli wearing a dramatic oversized white ruffled jacket paired with black leggings and gold glasses. 

    Hunter Schafer

    Hunter oozed feminine glamour in a plunge-neck asymmetrical gown with a gold statement choker necklace and Schiaparelli's signature toe heels.

    Sabrina Elba

    Victoria Beckham told us that cropped trousers are going to be 2024's most elegant trouser trend, and it looks like she was right. Sabrina looked effortlessly chic in a white two piece featuring cropped white trousers and a matching cape.

    Sabina Jakubowicz

    Model Sabina wore a fitted red maxi gown with flattering ruching and a haute hood.

    Naomi Campbell

    Naomi Campbell stepped out at Hotel Costes in a black jumpsuit layered with a longline velvet jacket.

    Carla Bruni

    Carla oozed sophisticated glamour in a contemporary penny coat featuring gold adornments and Schiaparelli's signature keyhole design. 

    Bella Thorne

    Bella Thorne wore black trousers paired with a jacket with statement sleeve and chunky gold buttons. She finished the look off with a Beyonce-approved wide-brimmed hat.
