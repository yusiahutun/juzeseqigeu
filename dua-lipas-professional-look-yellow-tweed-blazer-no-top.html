See Dua Lipa Professional Look with a Yellow Tweed Blazer and No Top

August 22, 2024
blog

Dua Lipa just brought a dash of fun to the typical blazer-and-jeans combo.

The pop star traveled to The Netherlands yesterday to attend the ESNS European Music Conference and Showcase Festival in Groningen, where she gave a keynote talk alongside her father Dukagjin on their Sunny Hill Foundation and annual music festival in Kosovo. For the engagement, during which she received the ESNS Excellence Award, the star brought some of her trademark edge and inventiveness to her business casual look, skipping a blouse to show her abdomen under a pastel yellow blazer.

She wore the tweed jacket with its single button fastened at the bottom of the lapel, paired with high-waisted jeans that featured distressed patches adorned with silver metallic thread for a fringe effect. Her accessories included a pair of blue denim kitten heels, spiked gold hoops, and a yellow shoulder bag, while her hair was styled in a bun with a swooping bang.

"First time back in Groningen, Netherlands for @esns.nl since 2016 but this time I came to proudly do a keynote with my dad @dukagjinlipa about our @sunnyhillfestival @sunnyhillfoundation," she captioned the post.

The Future Nostalgia singer, who was born in London to Kosovo Albanian parents, was named Honorary Ambassador of Kosovo last August. She took part in a ceremony with President Vjosa Osmani, in which the singer was honored for her humanitarian work and for raising awareness of the Republic of Kosovo.

"It’s an honour and a privilege to be able to represent my country all over the world and to continue my work and efforts globally to see that we leave our mark and make a difference," she wrote on Instagram at the time. "The youth of Kosovo deserves the right to visa liberalization, freedom to travel and to dream big. Thank you."
