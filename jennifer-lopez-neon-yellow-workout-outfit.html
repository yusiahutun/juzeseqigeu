Jennifer Lopez Makes Athleisure Luxe With Neon Yellow Workout Outfit

July 05, 2024
blog

Jennifer Lopez is known for her luxurious street style, which often features oversize silhouettes, vibrant colors, and mix-and-match patterns. But the multi-hyphenate’s gym style is just as worthy of the hype.

The “Let’s Get Loud” singer debuted her latest activewear look while out and about in Los Angeles yesterday. Lopez stepped out in a monochromatic neon yellow outfit consisting of a bright lemon-yellow cropped tank top with a plunging scooped neckline and a pair of yellow yoga pants with a black snakeskin pattern. She paired the bold look with chunky all-white sneakers, amber-tinted aviator sunglasses, and one of her signature accessories: a massive bling cup studded with glittering bronze rhinestones.

Lopez stepped out in another luxe workout ensemble last week. Photographed while leaving a gym in West Hollywood, the Shotgun Wedding star showed off her post-workout glow while wearing an all-black athletic outfit. It included a black triangle sports bra with spaghetti straps and straps criss-crossing across her chest, matching black yoga pants, and another pair of chunky white sneakers. She elevated the outfit with more of her go-to accessories, like a black Hermès Birkin bag and one of her trusty bling cups in glittering blue and gold.

The star has previously joked about how large her personal collection of bling cups is. During a Q&A video made for Coach, in which Lopez interviews herself, she asked, “How many bling cups do you actually have?” She laughed before answering herself, “Um … you don’t want to know.” Interviewer Lopez retorted, “No, you don’t know. You have so many that you actually do not know. It’s actually kind of crazy and pathetic.”
