2019 Old Settler's Music Festival Daily Lineups Announced

June 20, 2024
blog

Jason Isbell and the 400 Unit will return to the Old Settler's Music Festival in Tilmon, Texas, this April and now fans know when. He and Brandi Carlile top a list of performance times announced on Tuesday morning.

The Del McCoury Band, Shinyribs, Amanda Shires and Mondolin Orange are a few artists joining Isbell for the Friday night show (April 12) while Galactic, Hayes, Carll and Wild Child are a few of the dozen acts playing with Carlile on Saturday (April 13) of the April 11-14 roots, Americana and folk music festival. A press release points out that 2019 is the Old Settler's Music Festival's 32nd year.

Paul Thorn tops a five-artist lineup for Thursday night (April 11), while Shinyribs return for Sunday. There will also be a surprise gospel collaboration on Sunday morning. Mipso, the Lone Bellow and the Last Bandeleros are a few other artists slotted on the lineup. You can see the full lineup below.

The Tilmon, Texas fest is less than an hour from Austin, Texas, and San Antonio, just south of Lockhart, Texas. It's a lineup filled with Grammy nominees. Isbell has been nominated for four in years past, and Carlile is up for six at the 2019 Grammy Awards in February.

Tickets are on sale for the music and camping festival at the Old Settler's Music Fest website. Additionally, the fest announced that entries for the 2019 Youth Talent Competition will be accepted through Feb. 25. Last year's winner was Sarah Grace, who went on to compete on The Voice on NBC.
