Watch Charlotte Cardin's Paradise Motion Music Video

March 03, 2024
blog

Singer-songwriter Charlotte Cardin exudes a French girl cool factor (though she's from Montreal) seemingly in everything she does: her pared-down wardrobe, her blunt bangs hairstyle, and laid back performances. That effortlessness pervades in her music, even when she sings about trouble in paradise.

Cardin recently released a six-track EP, Main Girl, in September, which combines her soulful, raspy, Amy Winehouse-like vocals, a jazz-inspired retro sound, and an electronic influence. "Paradise Motion" is one that leans harder on that third factor, while telling the story of a two lovers who aren't quite on the same page.

"I'm upset and you're outside / And we're both stuck in our paradise," she sings in the chorus. Today, Cardin brings the track to life with a playful new music video, which largely consists of tour footage.

"The ‘Paradise Motion’ video was shot while we were on tour in the US," Cardin tells BAZAAR.com of the Sebastien Duguay-directed visuals. "We shot it between New Orleans and Atlanta and it's just an honest representation of the band vibe we have on the road with the ups and downs of that kind of lifestyle."

Cardin just finished touring with BØRNS, who's just released a new album of his own, Blue Madonna, but she's about to hit the road again with support from Niia.
