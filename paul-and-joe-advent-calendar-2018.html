Love cats AND makeup? We have found the ideal beauty advent calendar for you!

September 26, 2024
blog

Cat lovers, listen up…

    Christmas is the season of giving – we all know that. Buying gifts for your loved ones is great and everything, but sometimes, you have to extend that to yourself – and what better way to celebrate the festive period that an advent calendar? Chocolate is so ten years ago – it's all about the beauty offerings now, and with so many varieties to choose from, it's hard to pick a favourite. However, if you want something a little different to the rest, you have come to the right place. Are you a cat lover? Do you love kitsch beauty products? If you answered yes to either, (or both) we just know that you will be obsessed with Paul & Joe's Christmas creation, which combines the two. The Cadeaux de Noel Advent Calendar has 12 doors of delight – so best used from the middle of December, which will take you up until Christmas Eve. The £56 box is beautifully illustrated with Paul & Joe’s designer Sophie Mechaly’s cats - Gipsy and Nounette. What's more, when you open the box to reveal the doors, there's even a little button in the corner that meows. Yes, really!

    The Cadeaux de Noel Advent Calendar, £56, Paul & Joe

    Packed with festive favourites, it's the perfect offering to perfect your winter makeup just in time for the big day and will make counting down the time all the more enjoyable.

    The advent calendar features a selection of the brand's best products 

    The best thing about the brand is the incredible packaging – it's unlike any other as it is features pastel hues and delightful illustrations, with a distinctively boudoir vibe.

    The calendar available 1st November 2018

    The calendar is filled with a mix of full-size and miniature products, and our favourites have to be the pressed powder, hand cream, bold red lipstick as well as the special gift - an adorable card case.
