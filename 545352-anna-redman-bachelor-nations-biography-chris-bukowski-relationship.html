Who is Anna Redman?

Anna Redman was born on 29 May 1996 in Owatonna, Minnesota, USA, to Jerry and Michelle Redman. She is 27 years old as of 2024. Her zodiac sign is Taurus.

The reality TV star was raised alongside her two siblings, Jack and Lily Redman. Growing up, her parents nicknamed her Hollywood for her love for chic scarves and oversized sunglasses. Her sister Lily is a cheese connoisseur and a student at Creighton University.

Redman studied at Hinsdale South High School, where she was cheerleading squad captain. After completing high school, she enrolled at Loyola University in Chicago to study creative advertising and communication. She graduated from the institution in May 2018.

What does Anna Redman do for work?

Anna Redman has worked in advertising as a copywriter. She has worked with major brands like Coca-Cola, Moen, Smart Water, Carl's Jr., and Travelocity. She also worked for Havas Chicago.
