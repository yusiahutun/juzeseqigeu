Tory Burchs Rare Private Sale Is Finally Here, but Only for a Few Days

August 27, 2024
blog

I enjoy shopping, but I don’t do it often anymore, in that I’ve very much done a complete 360 from young Eva who would go to the mall after school nearly every other day to buy something to adult Eva who only shop for something when it’s really necessary — or, when when something that really bring immense value to my wardrobe. Let’s call it: mindful shopping. 

What’s more, seeing as I shop for a living, I know when the best sales usually roll around, so I save most of my energy (and money!) for those moments. One such shopping event that I eagerly, not-so-patiently count down for is… the Tory Burch Private Sale, which is happening right now, and just in time for my next vacation. How did you know, Tory? 

Small Kira Chevron Flap Shoulder Bag

Tory Burch’s pieces are top-quality, fit so well because of a *chef’s kiss* construction, and last long, wash after wash, and that’s why I’m willing to splurge on the designer label. However, the Private Sale, which yes, is technically “private,” though all you have to do to gain access is enter your email, is like my Super Bowl — sorry, I don’t watch sports, but I know this season was a good one thanks to Taylor Swift. That said, a sale of this magnitude doesn't happen often (twice  a year, to be exact), and it doesn’t last long either; the Tory Burch Private Sale officially ends February 26, so you have a few days to snag great deals on great clothing that will make you feel great.

Best Tory Burch Private Sale Deals 

There are more than 1,300 pieces on sale, which is a lot, though I would argue it’s not too much, either. Many brands unveil sales with thousands of things marked down, which makes picking out what you really want even harder. With Tory Burch’s humble 1,300 discounted pieces, things get a bit more digestible in the shopping department. However, I still decided to pull out the nine can’t-miss buys that you will love wearing now and come spring and summer, like these Eleanor flats that my co-worker saw me wearing one day and loved so much, she went to buy a similar pair a few hours later. They’re buttery soft, so they’re extremely comfortable, but they also look so elevated and chic. I've worn them with jeans and pleated midi skirts alike. 

Because a Tory Burch sale haul isn’t complete without a few of the designer’s iconic bags, you absolutely must add the famous Chevron style to your collection, stat. It typically costs nearly $500, but right now, you can get it for $270; basically, you can get two for the price of one, and, considering I’m a bargain shopper, these words are like music to my ears. 

Ready to check out more Tory Burch sale must-haves? Shop them below, but hurry, because prices go back up on Monday.
