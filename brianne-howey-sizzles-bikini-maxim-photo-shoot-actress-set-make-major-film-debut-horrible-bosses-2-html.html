Brianne Howey sizzles in bikini for Maxim photo shoot

There was nothing horrible about Brianne Howey in a red bikini in the November issue of Maxim magazine. 

The blonde beauty can be seen next month in her first major film role in the comedy Horrible Bosses 2 starring Jennifer Aniston, Jason Bateman and Jason Sudeikis.

Brianne was featured in the magazine's car issue that showed Dutch model Bregje Heinen on the cover standing up in a plunging black swimsuit inside a white convertible sports car.

Brianne sizzled in her two-piece swimsuit with her wet blonde hair slicked back and opened up to the magazine about working with major Hollywood stars in Horrible Bosses 2.

'It was scary, but you kind of find your groove. Seeing them in action is really inspiring. They are the real deal,' she said.

Brianne grew up in the eastern Los Angeles suburb La Cañada Flintridge and reflected on being raised close to Hollywood.

November issue: Brianne  was featured in the November issue that featured Dutch model Bregje Heinen on the cover

'I didn't necessarily want to be an actor. I wanted to be a flight attendant, because we traveled and moved around so much when I was little. When I saw View from the Top, Christina Applegate and Gwyneth Paltrow make being a flight attendant seem like the coolest job ever,' she said.

She also pointed out that she's no dumb blonde.

'I graduated college a semester early; I'm not so dumb,' said the New York University graduate.

Blonde beauty: Brianne is shown earlier this month at the premiere of The Judge in Los Angeles

Brianne also has had roles in the television shows Hart Of Dixie, Twisted and Red Scare and had a role in the sci-fi movie ETXR released online earlier this year.

'I've had a lot of roles where I play a good girl gone bad. I have a very sweet, all-American look, and then there’s a big reveal, where I'm walking down the street, swinging tequila,' she told the magazine.

She also has been cast in the short film SuperNovas and shared images of herself in a cheerleader costume earlier this month on the set.
