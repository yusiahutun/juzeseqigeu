A Walk-Behind Leaf Blower is Fall's Must-Have Yard Tool

August 31, 2024
blog

Autumn is undoubtedly one of the coziest, and most beautiful times of the year, with the golden brown leaves clenching onto the tree for the last time before they shed.

Trees evolved to shed their leaves as a way to reserve resources and protect themselves from being blown over in the harsher weather conditions. As beautiful as they are, once they’ve fallen from the tree, they can also be dangerous as they make the ground extremely slippery, which is why it’s important to gather them and either compost them or pile them out of the way.

Walk-behind leafblowers tend to be more powerful and stable than backpack leaf blowers. You won’t need to worry about the aches of lugging around a giant backpack. You have more control, using the handle to direct the machine up and down at your own pace.

Here is a list of our favorite walk-behind leaf blowers that will see you through many seasons.
