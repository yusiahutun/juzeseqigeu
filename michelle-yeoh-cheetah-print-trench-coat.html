Michelle Yeoh Shows Her Sartorial Claws in a Cheetah-Print Trench Coat

September 19, 2024
blog

Last year, when Michelle Yeoh enjoyed a streak of award show wins for her brilliant performance in Everything Everywhere All at Once, she pulled out a number of elegant ensembles, from her ethereal Dior dress at the Oscars to her midnight-blue Armani gown at the Golden Globes. Now, the Crazy Rich Asians star has returned to the red carpet to celebrate her new Netflix series, The Brothers Sun, and she’s gotten even bolder with her prints.

Gregg DeGuire//Getty Images

Yeoh arrived to the red carpet in a cheetah-print trench coat from Balenciaga’s Fall/Winter 2023 ready-to-wear collection. The belted, double-breasted outerwear features notched lapels that were folded up on the runway to give the effect of a high neckline, but Yeoh kept them down to show off her jet-black knit top underneath. Meanwhile, the Oscar winner also sported black denim pants and platform stilettos, which barely peeked out from underneath her long coat. She accessorized with jewelry from Jacob & Co.

ROBYN BECK//Getty Images

In The Brothers Sun, Yeoh plays Eileen, a.k.a. “Mama” Sun, the matriarch of the Sun crime family, who moves her youngest child to California to try to get him away from their family’s gangster activities. Her efforts prove futile when her eldest son comes tumbling back into their home and throws them into a lawless lifestyle once again.

Leon Bennett//Getty Images

On the red carpet, Yeoh cozied up with her costars, as well as Gwyneth Paltrow, whose husband, Brad Falchuk, is one of the producers on the series. For her appearance, Paltrow wore a red belted scoop-neck dress from the Cong Tri Spring/Summer 2024 ready-to-wear collection, which she paired with dramatic shoulder-duster earrings composed of geometric golden metal pieces.
