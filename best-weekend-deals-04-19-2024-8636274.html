Madewell, Veja, Lululemon, and Vegamour

September 05, 2024
blog

It might have only recently started to feel like spring, but a number of mid-season sales are already here; I’m definitely not complaining. 

If you’re looking for wardrobe staples to take you from spring into summer, Madewell’s sale-on-sale — where already-discounted items are now an extra 30 percent off with the code SPRING30 — is a must-shop. For trending shoes, there are few better deals than Rue La La’s Veja Sale, where the celebrity-loved sneakers are on rare discount, and if you want to transition your workout wear into warmer weather for less, Lululemon’s We Made Too Much has steals up to 31 percent off. Finally, Vegamour just launched its Spring Sale, and is offering 25 percent off its entire haircare lineup with the code SPRING25.

Shop our top picks from each sale, below.

68% Off: Madewell the Layton Midi Slip Skirt

Madewell Sale-on-Sale

During Madewell’s double-discount sale, you can save up to 68 percent on spring and summer staples with the code SPRING30. One of the best deals is on the brand’s timeless Layton Midi Slip Skirt, but this simple yet elevated white blouse is also just $28. Pair them together for a classically chic look that costs less than $60 (you’re welcome).

White pants are a warm-weather staple — and look good with everything from a summery blouse to a simple white tee — so make sure to grab this 100-percent cotton, wide-leg style while it’s on sale for just $56. And for those all-white looks, a pop of color is a must, which is why I’d recommend grabbing this orange leather bag while it’s 41 percent off.

22% Off: Veja V-90 O.T. Leather Sneakers

Rue La La Veja Sale

Rue La La just dropped a number of discounted Veja sneakers and all you need to access them is your email (yes, it’s free to sign up!). The brand, which has been worn by celebrities including Meghan Markle and Reese Witherspoon, rarely goes on sale — but right now, styles are up to 22 percent off, including the all-white V-90 O.T. Leather Sneakers. The V-90s are classically cool with a chunky silhouette, but if you favor something a little more streamlined, consider grabbing these Volley Suede Sneakers.

The V-12 Leather Sneakers have a similar simple silhouette but are available in a rich-looking black leather. And for something more cushioned and a touch more sporty, make sure to grab the Venturi Alveomesh and Suede Sneakers while they’re on sale for just $170.

31% Off: Lululemon Wunder Train Mesh Racerback Tank Top

Lululemon We Made Too Much

Lululemon’s We Made Too Much section is one of the best places to score steals on workout wear. This sports bra, which shoppers say “has fun look but also provides some extra lift and support,” is now just $54 while the classic Align Tank Top, which has more than 4,700 fans, is $49.

For something warm-weather ready, grab these 28-percent-off shorts that feature a 3.5-inch length, drawstring waist, and deep pockets. “I can wear these to workout and to run errands,” wrote one customer about the shorts while another said that they’re “extremely flattering.” Of course, you also can’t go wrong with a pair of classic leggings from the brand, and these are now just $99.

25% Off: Vegamour Gro Hair Serum

Vegamour Spring Sale

Vegamour, the haircare brand that’s been used by Nicole Kidman, Bella Hadid, and Mandy Moore just launched its Spring Sale, offering 25 percent off everything with the code SPRING25. If you’re new to the brand and looking to add thickness to your hair, consider grabbing its best-selling Gro Hair Serum. According to a shopper over 65 who “is post-menopausal and had Covid twice in the last two years,” this is the only product to not only stop their hair loss, but to promote new growth. “My hair stopped falling out within two months and small hairs started growing all over my head,” they gushed. Pair this with the Gro+ Advanced Replenishing Shampoo and Conditioner Kit for an extra boost.

My personal favorite product from the brand is its Hydr-8 Weightless Repair Oil, which softens my dry, overprocessed hair without weighing it down. Another InStyle-favorite is the Gro Brow Serum, which a beauty writer said helped their “thin ‘90s brows look so much fuller.”
