Man, 22, who collapsed and died outside club pictured as cops launch murder probe

June 24, 2024
blog

This is the first picture of a young man who tragically collapsed and died after an incident at a nightclub just days after Christmas.

Michael Lee, 22, collapsed outside Goldies Bar in Stockton-on-Tees, County Durham around 3.30am yesterday (Friday).

He had left the bar after an incident before becoming ill outside and then slumping to the ground.

Police and ambulance were scrambled to the scene as paramedics then rushed Michael to University Hospital of North Tees.

But he was sadly later pronounced dead at hospital.

Cleveland Police launched a murder investigation and two men, aged 27 and 30, were arrested on suspicion of murder.

They have both been released under investigation pending further enquiries.

Heartbroken friends and family today paid tribute to Michael, saying their "hearts are hurting" after his death.

Aidan Lee said: "God let me ask a serious question? Why in life do you want to keep snatching all our best people of us and leaving bad???

"R.I.P Micheal-lee my mush. We all love you always. Will always have.

"Your place will never be replaced mate.

"This 1 is going to take some healing now you're gone Charva.

"Lovee Big Broo Boo. Goodnite GodBless my mush til we meet and party on agen Xxx."

Elle Huskinson wrote: "Hearts are hurting. Things will never be the same.

"God really does take the best and we all love you so much, my thoughts are with the family."

David Bryson said: "RIP Michael Lee, heartbreaking news I heard earlier I just wish I could say a few things before you left us.

"I'll see you soon brother love you."

A spokeswoman for Cleveland Police confirmed yesterday: "Officers were called to Stockton High Street at around 3:20am this morning, Friday 27 December, where paramedics had been called to give medical assistance to a man who had collapsed in the street.

"The man was taken to the University Hospital of North Tees, where he was sadly pronounced dead.

"Police believe that the man had been inside Goldie’s Bar where an incident had taken place and he left the premises prior to becoming ill outside.

"Two men, aged 27 and 30, have been arrested on suspicion of murder and they remain in police custody at this time."

The North East Ambulance Service confirmed they were called the bar at around 3.14am after reports of an "unconscious patient" outside.

A spokesman for the service said they sent two ambulances and a specialist response car to the scene.

He said: "We received a call from a member of the public to say there was an unconscious person outside Goldies Bar at 3.14am.

"We dispatched two ambulances and a specialist car to the scene, however we did not transfer anyone to hospital."
