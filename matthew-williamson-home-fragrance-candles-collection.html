Matthew Williamson on his new luxury home fragrance candle collection

February 17, 2024
blog

Today, renowned British designer Matthew Williamson launches his debut home fragrance collection, a line of luxury candles and diffusers in his vivid signature style.

This move from fashion into fragrance, Williamson exclusively tells Bazaar, marks his new era as a designer.

“I’ve now shifted that focus and I’m really enjoying a new direction,” he explains. “There’s many overlaps and similarities in the processes between the two industries. I find spending time on an interior project more rewarding than the faster pace of more disposable aspects of fashion.”

Courtesy

Williamson has been influential in the interior space for some time, having worked on prior home furnishing projects, and he notes that this home fragrance collection has been a long time coming. “I’ve had it in my mind how the range might look and smell and how I would like to develop it into a full rainbow of product.” Naturally, the designer’s famed use of kaleidoscopic colour combinations features heavily – along with intricate patterns – but getting the fragrances spot on was of equal importance to him.

“Before starting I was lucky enough to chat to Jo Malone and ask her for some advice. She said, ‘keep it super personal and that will enable you to tell a unique story no one else can’. I thought that was such good advice and it’s been in my mind throughout the whole process.”

Williamson reveals he worked “with the two best fragrance houses in Paris”, and presented the perfumiers with clear visions for five scents, plus “reference images and paintings I’d imagined for the containers and a list of scents which three of the most important women in my life love”. He combined these elements with the technical expertise of the perfumiers to create the unique scents.

The collection comprises of a set of 200g candles (£49), 200ml room diffusers (£55) and 600g luxury ceramic candles (£140) in five scents inspired by Williamson’s global travels, though his charming homeland also made the cut in the form of English Garden.

Williamson’s personal favourite in the range? Jaipur Jewel. “It’s based around my trips to India. I’ve been about 40 times in all so I can safely say it’s a country I love and am passionate about. The scent is reminiscent of my time there. It also has an intense incense like aroma which was an ingredient taken from the cologne I wear myself.” He tells us that he burns this candle in his London lounge in the evenings, while Palm Springs scents his bathroom. “I have a home in Deia, Mallorca too and there I use Summer Siesta, a scent which I based on the coastal area containing lemons, mandarin oranges and white jasmine flowers.”

Up next he’s working on three new scents, plus travel sets and “a really innovative product we are at prototype stage with”. Can we even expect his signature scent, bottled? “One day I’d love to round it all off with an MW fragrance to capture the true DNA of my brand,” he reveals. Here’s hoping.

Available now at Harrods and Amara.com, prices range from £49 to £140
