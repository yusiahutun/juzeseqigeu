100+ most common Ghanaian last names and their meanings

June 03, 2024
blog

Ghanaians have beautiful names that are distinct and beautiful to the ears. The West Africans are also proud of their naming systems and will proudly choose to use their surname over other names. The most common Ghanaian last names tell an individual's place of birth, the time they were born, the part of the country they come from, and the traits their parents hoped they would acquire.

Do Ghanaian have surnames? Yes, a majority of Ghanaians have surnames which are used with Christian or western names. A Ghanaian surname will tell you which part of the country an individual is from, the languages they speak and the customs they follow.

Like many other African countries, Ghanaian surnames are distinct in particular parts of the country. Akan people, for instance, have different surnames from other tribes like the Asante or Mossi people.

Ghanaian last names with meaning

When naming their children, Ghanaian parents give them names that reflect positive traits. Parents hope for their children to be strong, intelligent, kind, and sharp, among other wonderful traits. Last names are also used to keep the legacy of the family going.
