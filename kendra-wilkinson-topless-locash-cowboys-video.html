Kendra Wilkinson Clears Up Rumors About Going Topless in LoCash Cowboys Video

March 06, 2024
blog

LoCash Cowboys recently fell victim to the famous saying, "Don't believe everything you hear." Following their recent 'C-O-U-N-T-R-Y' video shoot with reality TV show star Kendra Wilkinson, rumors began flurrying that the country pair was disappointed when the sought-after blonde refused to strip down to her birthday suit and expose herself for the final scene.

LoCash took to their Twitter page Monday afternoon to try to do damage control, writing, "Ha we didnt ask her to get naked, just [wear] a bikini. She was fun to work with."

'The Girls Next Door' and 'Kendra on Top' star also tweeted about the false stories, hoping to clear the air. "Get the truth about what went down!," Wilkinson wrote on her Twitter page, linking to a blog on her website. "Love @locashcowboys — they're awesome & I can't wait for you guys to see the video!"

On her official website, Wilkinson posted a photo of herself posing with LoCash's Preston Brust and Chris Lucas, and once again praised the duo, saying she enjoyed her time working with them.

"I just wanted to clear something up about the video I did with the LoCash Cowboys," she wrote in the post. "Some things were taken out of context about the video and I just wanted to let you all know that nothing happened! LOLOL Nobody wanted me to strip down! LOLOL The video was amazing, and I LOVE these guys! We had a great time, and I can't wait for you guys to see it!"
