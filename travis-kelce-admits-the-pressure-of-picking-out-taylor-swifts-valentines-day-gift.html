Travis Kelce admits the pressure of picking out Taylor Swift's Valentine's Day gift

September 28, 2024
blog

Travis Kelce is feeling the pressure of Valentine’s Day. It is really not the simplest task to pick out a gift for the number one pop musician of our generation, who may or may not have started writing songs about her sports icon beau (already), so the pressure is now on Trav to return the sweet gesture.

Valentine’s Day is special for Taylor Swift fans, especially after the singer acted in the movie of the same name; a romantic comedy along with her ex-boyfriend Taylor Lautner. The songstress, known for her personal connection with Swifties, also surprised fans in 2015 by inviting one lucky super fan to her apartment for the day.

In between creating a discography of all the stages one goes through while falling in and out of love, and making the day special for her ardent followers, Taylor Swift could be the mascot of Valentine’s Day. This time, fans are hoping that the celebrity would also get to spend the day feeling cherished with boyfriend Travis Kelce.

Fans can relate to Travis Kelce’s pressure about Valentine’s Day

In the latest podcast episode of Travis Kelce and Jason Kelce’s New Heights, the Catching Kelce star talks about Valentine’s Day briefly, while announcing their partnership with shopping platform Etsy. The Kansas City Chiefs player asked what sort of gifts Jason will be getting for wife Kylie for the special occasion.

Jason was honest about going the traditional route every year, resorting to chocolates and flowers. While they could be considered cliche, there is no denying that Valentine’s Day comes with a lot of pressure. Travis agreed with the sentiment, urging fans to check out Etsy if they are also feeling the ‘pressure’ like Jason and him.

Travis and Taylor’s ‘scheduling’ conflicts for Valentine’s Day

Finding the perfect gift is not the only hustle for Traylor. Sources close to the celebrity couple claim that scheduling conflict is another major thing that both the music legend and Travis have to navigate.

“They both plan to be with each other on Valentine’s Day, they just don’t know if it will be overseas or in America,” the insider told the Daily Mail.

“Some pieces have to fall into place and if one of those pieces is Kansas City making the Super Bowl, though it would be extremely tiring, she plans to come back for the Super Bowl for him if he is in the game,” the insider offered, adding, “They have tentative plans on what they will actually be doing, but it all will figure itself out once Kansas City is either in the Super Bowl or not. But they highly anticipate they will be together on Valentine’s Day.”
