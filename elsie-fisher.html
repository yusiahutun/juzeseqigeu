Elsie Fisher is an American actress hailing from the United States of America. Elsie is prominently known in the showbiz industry for her role in the comedy-drama film ‘Eighth Grade’ which was released in the year 2018 and has also lent her voice in several animated movies as well.

Early Life

Elsie Fisher was born on April the 3rd in the year 2003 in Riverside, a town in California in The United States of America. She was raised by her father Christopher Fisher alongside her brother Nathan Fisher. Unfortunately, information about Elsie’s educational qualifications is still unknown.

Elsie Fisher Career

Elsie Fisher started her career in showbiz at a very tender age of six years. Her first appearance came in the year 2009 in an episode of the supernatural drama series ‘Medium’ aired on NBC. She later went on to voice the nominal role for the English dub of a Russian animated series which goes by the name ‘Masha and the Bear’. This was between the year 2009 up until the year 2012. Perhaps the voice of Agnes from the animated series ‘Despicable Me’ might ring a bell. To not much of a surprise, the voice was Agnes was also by Elsie. She voiced Agnes in ‘Despicable Me 2’ as well. These movies were released in the years 2010 and 2013 respectively.

Perhaps one of her best and most prominent roles was that of a socially struggling teenage girl named Kayla Day in the comedy-drama film ‘Eighth Grade’. This was a directorial debut for well-known comedian Bo Burnham. Elsie received a lot of praise and support for playing her role in this film.

Fame

This Performance helped Elsie receives several Awards including a nomination for one of the most acclaimed awards i.e. the Golden Globe Award. Elsie was nominated under the category of Best Actress – Motion Picture Comedy or Musical. Following this, Elsie went on to voice a character named Parker Needler in an animated dark comedy film The Addams Family which was released in the year 2019. She has also made an appearance in the second season of ‘Castle Rock’. Elsie has won several awards some of which being for The Best Young Actress and Best Actress in a comedy.
