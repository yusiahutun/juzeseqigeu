Jackie 'O' Henderson is told she can't bring her puppy to work anymore

February 08, 2024
blog

Jackie 'O' Henderson was left mortified on Friday when her KIIS FM boss told her she couldn't bring her new Cavoodle puppy Sadie to work anymore.

The radio presenter looked horrified when her boss Tony Aldridge said the dog was too distracting and was causing damage to the studio carpets.

In hidden-camera footage, a horrified Jackie clutched onto Sadie as Tony delivered the bad news that she couldn't bring her into the office.

'Today has sort of come to a bit of a head with doggy coming in,' he said, as Jackie looked very worried. 

'I caught Ella [Kanna, digital content producer] not doing her job because she was looking after the dog. She then passed it to Josh [Fox, video producer] who was supposed to be doing a video.

'He then passed it to Mayo [guest booker Jaimee Blazquez]. Mayo then went off walking when we were on calls. None of them was focusing or doing their jobs. 

'And I've just got to the stage of going... they're all just distracted.'

A stunned Jackie then burst out laughing and asked her boss if he was joking, to which he replied 'no'. 

'Oh, my God... okay,' she said, nervously patting Sadie. 

Tony then told her the station had been slapped with a $950 bill to clean the carpets because of her dog.    

'I guess you are staying at home, bubba,' Jackie muttered as she kissed her pooch. 

'I didn't bring her in yesterday, on Thursday,' she added, to which Tony responded: 'Thursday was a good day... because she wasn't here, I can't have dogs in the workplace.' 

Jackie then insisted she did not know dogs weren't allowed in the office. 

At that moment, Tony revealed it was all just a hidden-camera prank as he burst out laughing and said he was 'only joking'.
