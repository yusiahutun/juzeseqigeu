Megan Fox is constantly changing up her appearance, experimenting with several different hair colors already this year. She’s stripped back on her Instagram, posting a no make up selfie, and fans can’t get over the fresh look.

The Hollywood star stripped back after a fun-filled weekend at Coachella, in which she added some impressive 26 inches of extensions to her new blue bob. It was time to go back to basics for Megan Fox, after removing her extensions, and her make-up for a stunning new selfie.

Megan Fox posts rare make-up free selfie and fans can’t believe it

Posting to her 21.8 million Instagram followers, the Jennifer’s Body star took a classic mirror selfie, as she chilled out in a black bra and pajama bottoms.

Looking like she was having the most relaxing day, the 37-year-old posed fresh-faced, without the usual glam fans are used to seeing her with.

“Repairing my hair (and 26 inches of extensions) post Coachella,” she wrote in the caption, before tagging Wella Professional Hair Care. However, it wasn’t her hair that fans were focused on.

“I didn’t know who the hell I was looking at until I read the account name!!” exclaimed one, claiming the star was unrecognizable.

“This does not look like Megan Fox AT ALL. AI doing its thing again,” wrote another.

A third questioned: “Is Megan Fox in the room with us?”

“How tf she get even more beautiful to the point that she doesn’t even look like herself anymore???” penned another.

However, others couldn’t get over her stunning new hair: “IN HER KING KYLIE ERA WITH THE BLUE HAIR!”

It’s not the first time the star has shocked fans with a ‘new look’ this year

In February, she and Machine Gun Kelly were seen out partying after the Super Bowl with Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce.

However, instead of ‘fangirling’ over the dream double date, fans couldn’t get over how different the star looked. She ended up seeing the comments, and ‘fought back.’

“Oh my god, guys, look how different I… don’t look at all,” she captioned photos of the group on the ‘gram.

“Turns out it was just a shadowy cell phone pic of me looking like a Ukrainian blow-up doll. When in REALITY I look like one of those super expensive silicone real s** dolls you can only get in Japan.” 

Unfortunately for Megan, she received backlash once again for her comments and once again, had to defend herself.

“It’s very rude of you to speak like that about Ukrainian women, that’s how you showed yourself,” one user responded, which received a reply from Fox herself. “Dear God. That is NOT what I meant.

“Ukrainian women are hot af and so in my imagination, the blowup dolls would also be hot. Let a girl make a joke for f**** sake.”
