Water-Loving Dogs Welcome at Pooch Plunge

March 17, 2024
blog

When Sailfish Splash Waterpark closes for the winter, local water-loving dogs come out to play, swim, and splash. The Stuart waterpark will set the scene for the Humane Society of the Treasure Coast’s tenth annual Pooch Plunge, in partnership with House of Hope, on December 9, from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m.

Admission is a $10 donation per dog, which benefits the humane society’s shelter animals. The event is open to dogs of all sizes and breeds. Online pre-registration is recommended and available here.

Guests also are encouraged to bring dog or cat supplies for the four-legged clients at the House of Hope. The nonprofit’s volunteers will be collecting pet toys, dog leashes, cat litter, dog and cat treats, and pet bedding.

In addition to the open swim for dogs, the event will feature pictures with Santa, music, a 50/50 raffle, food trucks, and more.

For more information about this splash-tacular event, contact Community Events Specialist Alyssa Bean via email at Abean@hstc1.org or call (772) 600-3215.
