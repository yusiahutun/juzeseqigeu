Early life

Rachel Anne Maddow was born on 1 April 1973 in Castro Valley, California, United States. Her parentsare Robert B. Maddow (father), a former US Air Force captain, and Elaine née Gosse (mother), a school programme administrator. Maddow has an older brother named David Maddow.

The author grew up in the San Francisco Bay area in a strictly Catholic, conservative household, despite her paternal grandparents being Ashkenazi Jews. Rachel Maddow's education has inspired many of her followers over the years.

She attended Castro Valley High School and later enrolled at Stanford University to complete her Bachelor of Arts degree in Public Policy and graduated in 1994.

In 1995, Maddow received a Rhodes scholarship to attend Lincoln College, Oxford, for postgraduate studies. In 2001, Rachel received a doctorate (D.Phil.) in Political Science from Lincoln College, Oxford. She presented her dissertation titled HIV/AIDS and Health Care Reform in British and American Prisons.
