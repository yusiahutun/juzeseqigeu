Ashton Kutcher tells Conan O'Brien he always brings a prosthetic penis everywhere with him

March 26, 2024
blog

Ashton Kutcher carries an enormous prosthetic penis with him at all times.

The Two And A Half Men star told Conan O'Brien on Wednesday night that he keeps the phallic object in his trailer.

His co-star Jon Cryer revealed Kutcher volunteered the information when writers for the show were trying to come up with a way of having him naked in studio for his debut on the comedy series.

Packing: Ashton Kutcher told Conan O'Brien on Thursday he always has a huge prosthetic penis close at hand

'In the very first episode that Ashton was in, there was a point in which he was supposed to drop trou and be completely naked in front of me,' he said. 'And we didn't know how to do that in front of a live studio audience without him actually being naked, because the whole, point was it was supposed to shock people. So we said we were going to have to cover him up somehow, but it still has to be shocking.

'We we're working this over in the writer's room, and then Ashton pipes in "oh, well I've got a huge prosthetic penis in my trailer"' 

'We all had that awkward moment of "of course you do",' 49-year-old Cryer recalled.

It was this big: Two And A Half Men co-star Jon Cryer said the member was 'huge'

Volunteer: The 37-year-old claimed he was the original mould for the enormous prop

'Doesn't everyone?' the 37-year-old chimed in. 

When Cryer went on to describe the prop as 'a flesh-colored full midsection with a - very impressive - anatomically correct member', Ashton replied: 'Listen, they only had one guy who was willing to be the mould,'... and raised his hand.

The Punk'd star then explained the fake penis had been made for a hidden camera web sketch series he used to be part of, for a short called 'naked man'.

Naked: The actor revealed the penis prop was used for a series of web sketches he directed, in which they sent a 'naked' man to a 'interesting locations'

In it, they would send an actor around the streets of LA 'fully naked', wearing the phoney phallus, at one point even sending him to buy ice-cream from a truck in Compton.

'People are chucking chairs at him off of their porch, and it was really funny! Less for him, more for us.

'Who knows when you're going to need a prosthetic penis? so I kept it,' he admitted. 

Thinking ahead: The Punk'd star had a feeling a need for a prosthetic penis would arise

'You don't throw something like that out,' Conan agreed.

'Why would you?!' Kutcher exclaimed.

Two And A Half Men's very last show airs on Thursday after 12 seasons on the air, with Kutcher having replaced Charlie Sheen in the final four seasons.
