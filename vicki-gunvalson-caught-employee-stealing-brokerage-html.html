Real Housewives of Orange County's Vicki Gunvalson has called in the police after thousands of dollars went missing from her business.

The 54-year-old reality TV star noticed huge discrepancies in the books of her insurance brokerage, Coto Financial, and suspected an employee was responsible, according toTMZ.

She went to the police after gathering evidence that the money had been embezzled.
