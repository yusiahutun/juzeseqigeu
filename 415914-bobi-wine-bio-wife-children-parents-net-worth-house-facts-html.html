Bobi Wine biography

Robert Kyagulanyi Ssentamu was born on February 12, 1982, in Nkozi, Uganda. The name of his father is Jackson Wellington Ssentamu, while that of his mother is Margaret Nalunkuuma. His mother was the breadwinner of the family, and she worked as a nurse.

Bobi Wine's family also includes his two siblings. He has two brothers Eddy Yawe and Mikie Wine. He spent most of his early childhood years in Kamwokya slum in the northeastern part of Kampala, the capital city of Uganda.

Robert Kyagulanyi's education

For his early childhood education, he enrolled at the Kitante Hill School, where he attained his Uganda Certificate of Education in 1996. He proceeded to Kololo Senior Secondary School for his high school education, where he attained his Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education in 1998.

After completing his secondary education, he enrolled for a diploma course in music, dance, and drama at Makerere University and graduated in 2003. In 2016, he enrolled at the International University of East Africa (IUEA) to pursue a degree course in law.

Political career

Music aside, Bobi Wine is also a famous politician. He debuted his political career in 2017 when he declared his interest to run for the parliamentary seat in Kyadondo County East constituency. He will be remembered for his unique style of campaigning door to door that allowed him to win the election.

He defeated the other two candidates, Sitenda Sebalu of the ruling National Resistance Movement Party, and Apollo Kantiniti of Forum for Democratic Change, with a wide margin. On July 24, 2019, he declared his intention to run for the highest office in the land, that of the presidency. To promote his presidential bid, he joined the National Unity Platform Party.

He faced President Museveni during the 2021 Ugandan General election. Unfortunately, he lost to Museveni, but he vowed to remain active in Ugandan politics. He has garnered a huge fan base in the country, especially from the youth and the people hailing from the 'ghetto' because of his keen concern for the needs of the average citizen.
