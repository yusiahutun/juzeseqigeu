3. What are the main ingredients used in Aldi’s gluten-free breads?

Aldi’s gluten-free breads are typically made with a combination of gluten-free flours such as rice flour, tapioca flour, and potato starch. These ingredients help to create a texture and taste similar to traditional bread while being free from gluten.
