Katie Holmes is bringing Khaite's cult knitwear into 2020

September 04, 2024
blog

It's the autumn must-have you never knew you wanted (or that it even existed) until Katie Holmes sparked a shopping flurry last year after being spotted wearing a cashmere bra.

The actress was seen in New York hailing a taxi wearing an understatedly chic, yet comfortable, outfit, a matching beige cashmere bralette-and-cardigan combo from Khaite that swiftly sold out within an hour – and went on to become one of the most-searched for pieces of clothing of 2019.

Pairing her cashmere with staple jeans and oversized sunglasses, it was the perfect example of transitional dressing from summer into autumn, with her cardigan worn off one shoulder to allow a glimpse of the co-ordinating bra.

The look quickly became a social media hit, with many sharing on their Instagram and tweeting about her outfit, while searches for brand increased by 217 per cent after she was seen wearing it and the bra is still Khaite's most viewed item ever – and it has, of course, led to countless high-street takes on the trend.

And now, it appears that Holmes may have done it again. Yesterday, the actress stepped out in New York City looking effortlessly chic and transitional-appropriate after shopping in Khaite's knitwear section once again, this time choosing a strapless white knitted top, which she paired with loose-fitting jeans and white trainers.

Jose Perez/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

The low-key look was another perfect example of how to wear knitwear in September and gently bring your wardrobe into the new season.
