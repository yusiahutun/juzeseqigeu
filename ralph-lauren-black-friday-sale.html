9 best Ralph Lauren Cyber deals 2023, according to a shopping editor

August 17, 2024
blog

If you’ve been in the market for a slice of Ralph Lauren, we’ve got good news – their Black Friday 30% off sale is still going, ending midnight on Cyber Monday, and if ever there’s a time to splurge on some wardrobe staples, it’s now. The Cyber weekend can be overwhelming and having written about it for years, we at HELLO! have come to see it as the time to invest in classic pieces, and timeless fashion items you’ll wear season upon season. It’s about quality, not quantity, and that’s why shopping Ralph Lauren’s Cyber sale is about as good girl math as it gets. 

If you’ve been looking to expand your capsule wardrobe and have a few key items missing like a quilted jacket or wide-leg trouser, you’ll do yourself a much better service by investing in a slightly pricier item than a piece of fast fashion, and that’s where the Ralph Lauren Cyber sale earns its weight in gold. Ralph Lauren is the epitome of preppy style, and the sale pieces don’t disappoint, leaning into the bread and butter of Ralph Lauren style – cable knit sweaters, collegiate sweatshirts and oversized shirts. Considering there’s over 1,300 products in the Ralph Lauren womenswear sale (and over 2,500 if you include menswear and kids), we’ve highlighted some of the best of the sale to stop you having to scroll.

What to buy in the Ralph Lauren Cyber sale 

Our best piece of advice? Shop the things you’ve always wanted from Ralph Lauren, those classic, easily identifiable RL pieces that’ll be forever worn, like logo sweaters and puffa coats, and consider your back to the office wardrobe and invest in tailoring – blazers, trousers and shirts, including striped Meghan Markle style stripe shirts too. Don’t sleep on Ralph Lauren’s accessories on sale either, their bags always get rave reviews for being good value, as well as stylish. 

The Ralph Lauren pieces on sale to shop

  • Cable Wool-Cashmere Mockneck Jumper

    Why we love it: Cashmere at this price? It’s a no-brainer, especially such a classic crew neck style. It’s a relaxed fit, with dropped shoulders and a length designed to hit at the hip. I’d opt for the brown marl mix, a great piece to wear with cream, brown satin or denim.

  • Rib-Knit Cotton Cardigan

    Why we love it: I personally find cardigans more wearable than sweaters, and Ralph Lauren’s rib knit cardigan is ideal for throwing on over T-shirts, vests or even slinky dresses come the party season.

  • Oversize Fit Cotton Twill Shirt

    Why we love it: A classic Ralph Lauren shirt should be on everyone’s shopping list, as it really will see you from the playground to the office and everywhere else in between. Equally as informal as it is formal, this shirt is said to be super soft thanks its composition of cotton twill, and cut to be oversize – size down if you want a more fitted look.

  • Logo Graphic Fleece Mockneck Sweatshirt

    Why we love it: I’ve already waxed lyrical about my love of a varsity sweatshirt, and this is a luxurious version. It’s a fleece blend, so will be super warm, and a crest graphic crackle-printed on the centre front for style. I’d add a shirt underneath and wear with dark denim and Mary Jane flats for a cool, casual vibe.

  • Water Repellent Taffeta Coat

    Why we love it: Finding a stylish water repellent coat isn’t always easy but I think I’ve found the holy grail with Ralph Lauren’s quilted bomber jacket. I love the cute cropped length and the lightly padded texture makes it so much warmer than a standard bomber. It’s also machine washable, and who doesn’t love that?

  • Striped Double-Breasted Linen Blazer

    Why we love it: This blazer exudes the preppy Ralph Lauren look, with a smart pinstripe, linen jacquard and double-breasted closure. Wear with jeans or over dresses, or go full on smart with the matching wide-leg trousers, also on sale.

  • Floral Tie-Neck Georgette Dress

    Why we love it: Ralph Lauren’s ethereal maxi is the type of dress you can wear anywhere. If you have a winter occasion, the longer sleeves and maxi length are spot on for dressier events or you can wear with this season’s essential, a sweater vest, and chunky boots, for a pub lunch.

  • Polo ID Calfskin Saddle Bag

    Why we love it: Ralph Lauren’s leather accessories include this saddle shaped bag, and I love it as an everyday bag evoking Ralph Lauren’s equestrian vibe. The white colour elevates the look for the season and the gold hardware adds that luxurious touch that you’d expect from a designer label. Made from Italian calfskin, it has dual adjustable straps and a magnetic slide lock.

  • Fair Isle Puff Sleeve Jumper

    Why we love it: I'm a big fan of a fair isle jumper and this is a cute version with puff sleeves, a traditional pattern and a blue shade. Knitted with a blend of soft wool and cotton yarn, it looks soft and chic.
