Taylor Lautner is facing 'trouble' in airports thanks to his wife

January 27, 2024
blog

Taylor Lautner dated a Taylor and married another Taylor. But the confusion regarding all the Taylors is not only among fans, it is slowly affecting the Twilight star himself.

The 31-year-old Hollywood heartthrob was earlier vocal about how fans have been pronouncing his name wrong for twenty years in an interview this year. The celebrity revealed the right pronunciation of his surname is actually ‘Lowt-ner.’

But the surname mispronunciation is not the only confusing thing about Taylor or his spouse’s names.

It is definitely an interesting journey to be in a marriage with someone who shares both your name and surname, and the actor is recalling all the troubles that he has been facing these days.

Speaking of a major airport confusion with security, Taylor Lautner shared: “TSA, airport, they’ll be like which one of you is Taylor? And we’re like ‘both of us’, and they’re like ‘which one of you is Taylor Lautner?’, also both of us!”

The trouble does not end there as Lautner adds: “They’re like ‘Ehh this seems fishy’, we’re gonna send you to security.”

The airport is not the only place where the celebrity couple faces confusion. The movie star also revealed that receiving Amazon packages could be tricky, as they always risk opening each other’s surprise gift by mistake.

Inside Taylor Lautner’s marriage with Taylor Dome

Taylor Lautner’s dreamy relationship with Taylor Swift is known to Swifties. But the Abduction star did not stop at dating one Taylor. The Grown Ups 2 actor met Taylor Dome in 2017 and got engaged by 2021.

Recalling how his sister played Cupid, he shared: “My sister Makena actually introduced us. She called me and said, ‘Dude, I found your future wife. You need to meet this girl.’ And the rest is history.”

Along with the Twilight Saga star, his better half also gushed over their beautiful story on Instagram as she wrote: “Thank you for encouraging me when I felt like quitting. Thank you for all the meals you’d make me when I was too busy studying. Thank you for quizzing me and trying to pronounce medical terms and abbreviations.”

The celebrity couple got married on November 11, 2022, and celebrated a year anniversary recently as Taylor Dome Lautner wrote: “My bestest friend, happy 1 year wedding anniversary! Being your wife is my absolute favorite thing. I never thought I’d find a love like yours.”
