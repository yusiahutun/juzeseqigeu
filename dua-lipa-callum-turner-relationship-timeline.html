Dua Lipa and Callum Turner's Full Relationship Timeline

May 05, 2024
blog

This year kicked off with a new couple being inducted into the Hall of Hollywood Romances: Dua Lipa and Callum Turner.

The two Brits first sparked romance rumors in early January, after Lipa was spotted supporting Turner at the premiere after-party for his new Apple TV+ series, Masters of the Air. Since then, they have gone more public, with a streak of paparazzi- and PDA-filled dates.

Lipa was previously romantically linked to French film director Romain Gavras, while Turner was dating The Crown star Vanessa Kirby.

“Dating, I think overall, is just a little confusing,” Lipa recently reflected in an interview with Rolling Stone. “It’s either through friends of friends or people you trust where you can meet new people, because [dating] is not really so straightforward when you are, I guess, a public person.”

Ahead, a complete breakdown of Lipa and Turner’s relationship so far.

April 25, 2024: They have a date night at a gala.

BACKGRID

The couple enjoy a night out after attending the Time100 Gala in New York City. For the occasion, Lipa wears a draping black floral midi dress with sheer black tights, stiletto Mary Jane heels, and a long black coat. Turner, meanwhile, wears a suave navy blue suit.

March 26, 2024: They travel to London.

The couple are pictured arriving at a London station after taking a train from Paris. In the process, the two show off their complementary couple’s style. Lipa wears a sweeping leather coat with jeans, sneakers, and a decorated Hermès Birkin bag. Meanwhile, Turner wears a brown leather jacket over a sweater, dark trousers, and boots.

Neil Mockford//Getty Images

March 15, 2024: They enjoy a romantic breakfast and stroll in L.A.

Lipa and Turner enjoy a romantic breakfast in Los Angeles to kick off their day. In photos published by the Daily Mail, the two are all smiles as they gaze lovingly into each other’s eyes at a restaurant and later embark on a stroll, walking hip to hip with Turner’s arm around Lipa.

The singer looks casually cool in a black and yellow leather motorcycle jacket by Gucci, layered over an oversize pale yellow striped button-up shirt and white tank top, and paired with baggy jeans and chestnut Ugg slippers. She carries a spacious black leather purse, wears her fiery locks down, and appears to wear no makeup. The actor looks suave beside her in a black work jacket layered over a simple white T-shirt, black track pants with a white stripe going down each leg, and light gray sneakers.

March 2, 2024: They go to the Brit Awards after-party.

Lipa and Turner are three-for-three for award-show date nights. They are photographed together in the back of a car on their way to the post–Brit Awards bash, wearing big smiles and engaging in some PDA, with Lipa sweetly resting her hand on Turner’s thigh. The singer looks as cool as ever in a maroon leather motorcycle jacket, while the actor goes all-black in a bomber jacket and jeans.

Ricky Vigil M / Justin E Palmer

February 18, 2024: They go to the BAFTAs after-party.

Lipa and Turner attend the 2024 BAFTAs after-party. They walk to the venue hand in hand, coordinating in black, Lipa in a romantic floral lace gown with barely-there spaghetti straps and Turner in a classic suit and tie.

Neil Mockford / Ricky Vigil M

February 4, 2024: They go to the Grammys after-party.

Turner accompanies Lipa to an after-party for the 66th Grammy Awards. The two are seen exiting a car, Lipa in a backless long-sleeved black dress with exposed thong straps and Turner wearing a classic black suit.

BACKGRID

Lipa walked the red carpet solo earlier in the night.

January 31, 2024: They share a passionate kiss in L.A.

Lipa and Turner are spotted on a love-filled day out in Beverly Hills. The pair are seen walking around the city with their arms around one another, at one point stopping for coffee and sharing passionate embraces and kisses.

Both look casual in white tops and jeans for the outing. The singer opts for a white tank with black details, plus vintage-finish jeans and a woven black leather shoulder bag by Bottega Veneta. The actor chooses a cream-colored henley and light-wash blue jeans.

BACKGRID

January 16, 2024: They confirm their romance by going on a PDA-filled date.

More paparazzi photos emerge of the couple heading out for dinner in Hollywood. The snaps show Turner and Lipa affectionately smiling and embracing each other, and there are several shots of the two kissing.

Lipa and Turner even match for their date, both wearing collared black jackets.

BACKGRID

January 14, 2024: Dua and Callum head out for a date night.

Paparazzi capture their first pictures of Lipa and Turner enjoying a date night, in Santa Monica.

In the photos, Lipa wears a long black coat, a white collared button-down, creamy straight-leg pants, and metallic open-toe sandals. Turner wears a military-green knit turtleneck sweater, khaki cargo pants, and white sneakers.

January 11, 2024: A video of Dua and Callum slow dancing emerges.

TMZ publishes footage of Lipa slow dancing and seemingly sharing a kiss with Turner at the after-party for the Los Angeles premiere of Masters of the Air, immediately sparking speculation about their relationship status. Lipa’s presence at the event is presumably in support of Turner, who stars in the Apple TV+ drama series alongside Austin Butler, Sawyer Spielberg, Barry Keoghan, and Lauren McQueen.
