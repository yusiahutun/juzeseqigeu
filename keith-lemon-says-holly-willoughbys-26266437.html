Keith Lemon says Holly Willoughby's husband made him be 'bad' to her on Celeb Juice

May 03, 2024
blog

Keith Lemon joked that Holly Willoughby 's husband Dan Baldwin made him say "bad" stuff to her on Celebrity Juice.

Holly was one of the original team captains alongside Fearne Cotton, before they left the ITV show and were replaced by Laura Whitmore and Emily Atack.

Funny-man Keith – real name Leigh Francis – made an appearance on Lorraine and spoke to Christine Lampard about his television favourites.

At one point, the cheeky chap asked Christine if she was asking her own questions or if a producer was telling her what to say through her ear-piece.

Brunette beauty Christine said the questions were her own and nobody was telling her what to say – unless it was time to wrap it up to go to an ad break.

But Keith confessed that he often had producers telling him to say stuff on Celebrity Juice, going as far to say that Dan – who is a television producer on the show – told him to be "bad" to Holly.

He said: "When I do Juice there's often someone in my ear going, 'Say this.'

"Normally it's Holly's husband saying, 'Say this bad thing to my wife,' and I'm like, 'No way! I'm not saying that!'"

Holly got married to Dan in August 2007.

He is the co-founder of a media company and was a producer on Ministry of Mayhem, which Holly presented alongside Stephen Mulhern.

Dan is more recently an executive producer on Celebrity Juice.

The happy couple have two sons – Harry and Chester – and one daughter – Belle.

Meanwhile, Keith is married to Jill Carter and together they have two daughters – Matilda and Dolly.

Lorraine airs weekdays at 9am on ITV
