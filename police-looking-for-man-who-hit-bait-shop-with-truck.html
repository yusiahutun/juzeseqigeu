Police looking for man who hit bait shop with truck

May 07, 2024
blog

by: Staff

Posted: May 2, 2018 / 02:08 PM CDT

Updated: May 2, 2018 / 04:15 PM CDT

The Dixon Police Department is asking for the public’s help to identify a man whose truck pulling a trailer caused damage to a building.

The accident occurred at Bunny’s Bait Shop at 1024 East River Road. 

A man entered the business’ parking lot around 6:52 p.m. Tuesday driving a truck pulling a triple axel trailer. The make and model of the truck is unknown.

The man entered the self-serve area of the business and purchased items, then drove around the east side of the building and caused damage to the northeast corner of the building. 

The Dixon Police Department is asking anyone with information about the accident or who can identify the man to contact the Dixon Police Department at 815-288-4411 or Crimestoppers at 1-888-Caught-U (1-888-228-4488).
