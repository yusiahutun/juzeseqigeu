Who is Tupac Shakur?

Afeni was an activist and businesswoman. She passed away on 2 May 2016, reportedly due to cardiac arrest. She was married to Mutulu Shakur between 1975 and 1982 and had children with him. Mutulu was a well-known doctor, politician, and activist.

He died of bone marrow malignancy on 6 July 2023. Tupac had no close relationship with his biological father, William Garland, and that could explain why the popular rapper took his step-father's last name, Shakur.

Tupac began his musical career in 1991 with his debut album 2Pacalypse Now. He succeeded in building a very successful career in the music industry. To date, he is considered one of the most significant hip-hop performers in the genre's history.

Did Tupac have brothers? Tupac does not have biological brothers or sisters. He is the only child from his father's relationship with Afeni. However, he has several half and step-siblings. Here are their details.

Tupac's little sister now serves as the president and CEO of the Tupac Amaru Shakur Foundation. The foundation addresses mental health conditions and eradicates the effects of trauma in the community.
