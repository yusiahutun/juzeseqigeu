The list of top three kids with the highest level of Intelligence Quotient (IQ) in the whole world is dominated by three black geniuses.

Ramarni Wilfred, Anala Beevers and Alannah George who have the highest IQs ever in the world are currently considered to be the world's topmost geniuses.

1. Ramarni Wilfred

Ramarni has an IQ score of 162, higher than Albert Einstein’s and Stephen Hawking’s.

The 16-year-old boy, whose IQ is also higher than Bill Gates, was only 10 when he wrote a paper on the philosophy of fairness, Face 2 Face Africa reports.

2. Anala Beevers

At just four years old, Anala Beevers possessed an IQ of over 145. She could identify each letter of the alphabet when she was just 10 months.

In 2014 when she was five years, Anala could recite the name of every North American state and capital on the map.

3. Alannah George

Alannah, who has an IQ score of 140, is the youngest member of Mensa from the United Kingdom. The four-year-old girl has an obsession with words and numbers.

The little child, who taught herself how to read before starting school, prefers reciting the alphabet and times tables than singing nursery rhymes.

An IQ is a standard measure of human brain and intelligence using indicators such as a person’s reasoning capacity and application of information obtained in solving problems. Also measured are short and long-term memories, ability, and speed of solving puzzles.
