When in your routine should an acne spot treatment be applied?

Spot treatments can be applied during either your morning or evening skin routines, but many beauty enthusiasts most commonly use them in the daytime. "I personally use a spot treatment after applying moisturizer, but before applying sunscreen," Hartman says. "You can also spot treat right before applying moisturizer and sunscreen."

What are some red flags that an acne spot treatment is too intense?

According to Hartman, some clear indicators that a product may not be ideal for you are, "If your skin feels uncomfortable—too tight, painful, or tender to the touch—or is dry and flakey, your spot treatment may be too intense," he advises. "You can try using less of the spot treatment, or space out the times per week you use it, or try and entirely different product. Your board-certified dermatologist can help make recommendations on products and frequency of use, too."
