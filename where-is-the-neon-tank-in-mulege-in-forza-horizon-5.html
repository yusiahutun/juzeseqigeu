The Forza Horizon 5 Midnights at Horizon Festival Playlist is live, and with it comes a new photo challenge set in Mulege where you need to find the Neon Tank decoration. But where is the Neon Tank in Mulege in Forza Horizon 5?

If you’re struggling to locate the Neon Tank in Mulege Forza Horizon 5, our guide has everything you need to know to find this week's Photo Challenge location.

Where is the Neon Tank in Mulege in Forza Horizon 5?

As part of the Midnights at Horizon Series update, Mexico has been adorned with neon lights and decorations that light up the city during extended night periods. The decoration you need to find for the Photo Challenge is called the Neon Tank decoration. But how do you find the Neon Tank?

First, you need to travel to Mulege. This region is located southwest of the map below the main Horizon Mexico Outpost site.

Within Mulege, the Neon Tank is on the side of a yellow building close to the Casa Bella house.

Use the pointer on the map below to find the exact location of the Neon Tank:

You need to find the Neon Tank for the Midnights at Horizon Photo Challenge.

This Photo Challenge requires you to snap a photo of any Modern Muscle car with Neon Tank in Mulege. Filtering Modern Muscle cars in your collection will show a list of cars you can use in this Photo Challenge. We used the 2015 Ford Mustang 2015.

Park next to the yellow building with the Neon Tank and snap a photo. You will then be rewarded with the Haunted House Car Horn.
