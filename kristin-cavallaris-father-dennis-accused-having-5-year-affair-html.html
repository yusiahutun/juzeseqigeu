Strain shows on downbeat Kristin Cavallari after father Dennis is accused of conducting a five-year long affair

With her engagement to Chicago Bears star Jay Cutler back on, Kristin Cavallari should be over the moon.

But the 25-year-old reality star’s happiness might be crushed by reports that her father Dennis Cavallari has been leading a double life.

And today stepping out with her blonde hair scraped back, no make-up and a drab black dress, it appears the strain of the revelations of her father's alleged five year long affair is already beginning to take its toll.

Strained: It has been alleged that Kristin Cavallari's father Dennis has enjoyed a five-year affair with a woman from Orange County

According to Star magazine Kristin’s father has been having a five year long relationship with a woman named Tracy Sill.

Kristin's appearance was in marked contrast to the glam figure she cut yesterday while seen out and about in her native LA.

From fab to drab: The reality TV star appeared strained as she stepped out amid the revelations yesterday, but seen right, the day before - she appeared her usual sunny self

The Orange County resident told the magazine: ‘He told me he loved me and even lied about not being married in a desperate attempt to continue our relationship.’

Big claims: Dennis Cavallari has been accused of having a five-year affair

‘When Kristin discovers the truth, she will see her father for what he really is: a philandering, sex-crazed cheat.’

The magazine claims to have seen emails and text messages from Dennis to Tracy and reports most were ‘too sexually charged to print’.

Ms Sill also added: ‘He told me he was addicted to Viagra and marijuana. He talked about us being together, moving to Florida and having a future.’

Dennis is currently married to Nicole King Cavallari, who is Kristin’s step-mother.

The magazine reports Tracy ended the relationship when she learned Dennis was married but reached out to Nicole to blow the lid on her relationship with Dennis.

She branded the Cavallaris: ‘A dysfunctional family full of lies and deceit.’

Ruining the happiness? Kristin, who is set to marry Jay Cutler, has not spoke out about the claims made by Tracy Sill on her Twitter page

A spokesperson for Kristin was unavailable for comment earlier today and the bride-to-be has made no reference to the story on her Twitter page today

Kristin confirmed her engagement to Cutler was back on last month and was said to be delighted.

The sports star first proposed in April 2011 and Kristin had busied herself for a summer 2012 wedding.

But the couple split shortly after their engagement party in July before getting back together at the end of last year.
