Amboseli Institute of Hospitality and Technology: Courses, fee structure, and application

April 12, 2024
blog

Amboseli Institute of Hospitality and Technology (AIHT) is a well-known institution offering top-quality hospitality education. Its teaching and non-teaching staff are committed to ensuring students get the highest education standards with minimum disturbances. The institution, whose mission is to equip students with quality practical skills in a professional and supportive learning environment, offers many courses at an affordable price.

Their initial target market was form four leavers with a KCSE certificate. However, with an increased demand of their courses, they have expanded their opportunities to include advanced level learners, those who are working but are willing to take refresher courses and youth groups and women groups looking for short personalized courses fine tuned to their liking.

School of Hospitality

The School of Hospitality AIHT's flagship project. It aims to impart skills and knowledge to the ever-growing and dynamic hospitality industry.

They train students to work in but are not limited to, hotels, bakeries, apartments, members-only clubs, cruise ships, airlines, and hospitals. These courses focus on practical training and are progressive from craft to diploma levels. Some of its departments include:

  • Hotels and Resorts Management
  • Culinary Arts
  • Event Management

School of Tourism

The School of Tourism is design to equip learners with vital knowledge and hands-on skills necessary to thrive in this dynamic and constantly evolving industry. Courses offered here include:

  • Diploma in Tour Guiding and Administration
  • Diploma in Sustainable Wildlife Management
  • Diploma in Tourism and Business Studies
  • Certificate in Sustainable Wildlife Management
  • Certificate in Tour and Travel Management

How to apply in Amboseli?

Follow the steps below to apply to Amboseli.

  • Identify the program that best fits your career goals and aspirations.
  • Fill out the online Amboseli Institute application form thoroughly, ensuring all information provided is current and precise.
  • Review your application meticulously to confirm the accuracy of all details submitted.
  • Certain programs may necessitate completing a written assessment to gauge your qualifications and preparedness for the program.
  • Be prepared for a potential interview as part of the evaluation process to assess your compatibility and dedication to the program.
  • Following a comprehensive review of your application, assessment, and interview, a final determination will be made.
  • You will receive notification of the decision and any subsequent steps to be taken.

    • Entry requirements

    Below are the general entry requirements for joining Amboseli Institute.

    • Diplomas: Minimum entry requirement is C- grade in KCSE.
    • Certificates: Entry level is D plain in KCSE.
    • Artisan level: Entry requires D- grade in KCSE.
    • Working professionals: Need two years of documented working experience.
