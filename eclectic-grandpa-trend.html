If you spend any time on social media, you’d be hard-pressed not to notice the micro and macro trends that pop up frequently. Balletcore, cottagecore, coastal grandma, and many more have gone viral in recent months. And thanks to our friends at Pinterest, we can add a new 2024 trend to the list: the eclectic grandpa trend, or as TikTok will crown it, #grandpacore.

The eclectic grandpa trend is exactly what it sounds like: 2024 style stars are taking inspiration from stylish grandpas and emulating the expressive, eccentric personalities of these wise gems. Think retro streetwear, chic cardigans, and customized clothing. Really, wear anything retro or vintage-adjacent, and you’ll nail the trend.

After spending decades curating their wardrobes, eclectic grandpas look quirky yet sophisticated—mixing bold items with cozy, lived-in pieces that give off a tenured professor vibe. Gigi Hadid, in cozy knit cardigans and penny loafers, and Harry Styles, in kooky sweater vests, are the latest icons to test-drive the trend. And if you want endless inspiration, just head to Tyler the Creator’s Instagram for a plethora of eclectic grandpa hats, funky print sweaters, cargos, and tweed blazers.

To master this emerging aesthetic, you’ll need expressive, vibrant pieces made from quality, built-to-last fabrics. After all, grandpas have such incredible and unique style, because their closets are full of well-loved pieces they’ve looked after over time.

To achieve this style overnight is not the goal—nor is it possible. Instead, it’s about mixing what’s already in your wardrobe with a couple new pieces to eventually garner eclectic grandpa energy. As eclectic grandpas know well, longevity is key here.
