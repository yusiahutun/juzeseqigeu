Prince Harry will 'regret' leaving his family in tough year ahead, psychic predicts

February 11, 2024
blog

Exclusive: Celebrity Tidbit sat down with a psychic medium to get all the gossip on his predictions for 2024, and it looks like Prince Harry may begin to regret what’s gone down with the royal family over the past few years.

Prince Harry and Meghan Markle decided to up and leave the royal family in 2020, and move overseas, as they now reside in LA. In that time, they’ve taken part in interviews, documentaries, and the Duke released his tell-all memoir, Spare. These have all strained his relationship with his family, and celebrity psychic medium Adam Norton predicts he’ll start to regret his decisions this year.

Prince Harry ‘will regret his decisions,’ celebrity psychic says

Many critics have said Harry doesn’t look happy with Meghan Markle, and it looks like Adam thinks the same.

He tells us: “Harry I feel is putting on a front at the moment, and I don’t feel a lot of happiness around him yet. Not deep happiness, anyway.”

Since leaving the UK, the Duke has been back a few times, and the celebrity psychic predicts he’ll start to miss his hometown.

“I do feel he’ll miss the UK, and the fact he’s pretty much out of the royal family I actually feel deep down he’ll begin to regret this,” he predicts.

Since leaving The Firm, Harry’s relationship with his brother Prince William is said to be ‘severely strained,’ as well as his relationship with his father, King Charles.

However, Norton doesn’t think the feeling will be mutual, as he tells us to “expect there to be a bit of backlash [or] gossip about Harry [and] the royals to surface.”

He says he sees the royal family “throwing a dig” towards the Prince in 2024, so whether his regret turns into him coming back to the UK, only time will tell.

In terms of his relationship, Adam doesn’t predict that going down well either, as he actually sees a breakup between Harry and Meghan. He predicts the former Suits actress will go back to acting, and we may even see her partaking in some behind-the-scenes work.

The couple hope to ‘make amends’ with the King

Everyone has New Year’s Resolutions they’ll be hoping to stick to, and it looks like the Sussexes are no different.

Royal expert Ingrid Seward told GB News the couple “need the King and his blessing far more than the King needs them,” and feels they will be seeing him in the New Year.

Although Charles saw his son in 2023, he did not see his children, Archie or Lilibet in person. Things may change this year, but once again, only time will tell.
