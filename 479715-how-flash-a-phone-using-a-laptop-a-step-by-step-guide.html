Installing USB drivers

Download the relevant Android USB drivers from the internet. Using a Windows computer, you can connect your phone to the computer via the USB port. Open computer management and select device manager. In the device manager right pane, locate portable devices or other devices. Right-click the name of your connected device and select update driver software.

In the update hardware wizard, browse your computer, head to the downloaded folder, and install the driver.
