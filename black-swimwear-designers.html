6 Black Swimwear Designers to Have on Your Radar

August 20, 2024
blog

Andrea Iyamah Nisi Orange One Piece Swimsuit

Andrea Iyamah Nisi Orange One Piece Swimsuit

The Nigerian designer is known for her opulent use of color and texture. This orange one-piece is sure to make a statement no matter where you are. 

Jade Swim Apex One Piece

Jade Swim Apex One Piece

Founded by former fashion editor Brittany Kozerski, Jade Swim is known for striking minimalism. Jade Swim pieces are also sustainable, and the fabrics used offer UV protection. You'll reach for this asymmetrical one-piece all summer long.  

d.bleu.dazzled Crystal Cherry Triangle Bikini Top

d.bleu.dazzled Crystal Cherry Triangle Bikini Top

Destiney Bleu's brand is known for its striking crystal work and has been seen on It girls like Beyoncé and March BAZAAR cover star Megan Thee Stallion. The only thing better than being out in the sun? Sparkling in a Swarovski crystal two-piece.

Fe Noel Two Tone Swimsuit

Fe Noel Two Tone Swimsuit

In Brooklyn, Felisha Noel draws inspiration from her Grenadian heritage, the Caribbean, and vibrant color. She opened her first brick-and-mortar at just 19 years old. Her swimwear feels like a mirror reflection of her current collections, using the same colors and prints. 

MBM Swim Rebel

MBM Swim Rebel

Self-taught designer Marcia B Maxwell, or MBM, favors structured silhouettes and clean lines in her design work. Her swimwear offers a kind of instant elegance, and her suits will wow at any pool party. 

Dos Swim Kisa One Piece

Dos Swim Kisa One Piece

Shay Johnson and Paula Hess, founders of Dos Swim, struggled finding swimsuits that fit both their body types. With their brand, they aim to make swim that reflects their colorful style, while also paying careful attention to common fit problems. This gorgeous, colorful, cutout one-piece is sure to make heads turn.
